EGR Cooler/EGR Valve Replacement
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the IMRC valve.
  1. Remove the dipstick tube bolt (A), and the IMRC valve stay (B).
  1. Remove the nuts, and the EGR pipes (A) and EGR valve (B).
    NOTE: When installing the EGR pipe, replace the gaskets (C).
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Disconnect the hose (A).
  1. Remove the bolts, and the EGR cooler (B).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with the new O-ring (C) and gasket (D), then refill the radiator with engine coolant.
  1. With DPF system: Do the DPF regeneration.
