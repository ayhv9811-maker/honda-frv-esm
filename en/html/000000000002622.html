Emblem Replacement - Supplement
NOTE: When removing the emblems, take care not to scratch the body. Use dental floss to cut the adhesive safely.
  1. Clean the body surface with a sponge dampened in isopropyl alcohol. After cleaning, keep oil, grease, and water from getting on the surface.
