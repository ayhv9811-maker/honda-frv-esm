Glove Box Removal/Installation
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the bolts.
  1. While holding the glove box (A), release the glove box stop portions (B) on both sides from the dashboard by pushing them inside, then remove the glove box.
  1. Install the glove box in the reverse order of removal.
