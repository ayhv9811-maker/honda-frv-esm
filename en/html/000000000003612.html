Rear Seat Armrest Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the clip (A) from the left portion of the armrest shaft (B).
  1. Slide the armrest (A) toward the left side of the vehicle, and remove the pivot shaft (B) from the collar (C) on the right side of the vehicle by pulling back on the armrest, then remove the armrest.
  1. Install the armrest in the reverse order of removal.
