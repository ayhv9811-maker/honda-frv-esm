Timing Belt Auto-tensioner Inspection
  1. Remove the auto-tensioner.
    NOTE: Do not apply force to the tensioner pulley.
  1. Put the auto-tensioner on a surface plate.
  1. Measure the height at point A (A) and point B (B).
  1. Calculate the difference in height between point A and point B. If the difference in height is out of tolerance, replace the auto-tensioner.
    Difference In Height
    Specification:
    0.75 mm (0.030 in.)

  1. Inspect the spring grommet (A). If the grommet is damaged or deteriorated, replace the auto-tensioner.
