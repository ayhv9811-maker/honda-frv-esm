Headlight Replacement
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the connectors (A) from the headlight assembly (B).
  1. Remove the three mounting bolts, crip and the headlight.
  1. Remove the bolt (C) and the corner upper beam (D) from the headlight.
  1. Install the headlight in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After replacement, adjust the headlights to local requirement.
