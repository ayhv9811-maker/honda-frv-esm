Side Impact Sensor (Second) Replacement
Removal
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Disconnect the appropriate side curtain airbag 2P connector.
  1. Remove the trunk side trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the floor wire harness or right side wire harness 2P connector from the side impact sensor (second).
  1. Remove the Torx bolt (A) using a Torx T30 bit, then remove the side impact sensor (second) (B).
Installation
  1. Install the new side impact sensor (second) with Torx bolt (A) then connect floor wire harness or right side wire harness 2P connector (B) to the side impact sensor (second).
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. After installing the side impact sensor (second), confirm proper system operation: Turn the ignition switch ON (II); the SRS indicator should come on for about 6 seconds and then go off.
  1. Install all removed parts.
