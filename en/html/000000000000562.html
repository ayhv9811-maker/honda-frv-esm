DTC Troubleshooting: 12, 14, 16, 18
DTC 12, 14, 16, 18:
 Wheel Sensor (Electrical Noise/Intermittent Interruption)
NOTE: If the ABS and other indicators come on because of electrical noise, the indicators will go off when you test-drive the vehicle at 15 km/h (9 mph) or more and noise is gone.
  1. Check the DTCs using the HDS.
Are DTC 11, 13, 15, and/or 17 indicated with DTC 12, 14, 16, and/or 18 at the same time?
YES -
Troubleshoot DTC 11, 13, 15, and/or 17, and recheck.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Check the appropriate wheel sensor and magnetic encoder on the wheel bearing.
