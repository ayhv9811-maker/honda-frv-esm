M/T Shift Lever Housing Replacement
  1. Remove the instrument middle panel.
  1. Remove the instrument center lower cover.
  1. Remove the shift lever housing mounting bolts.
  1. Remove the clamps (A).
  1. Remove the select cable (B) and shift cable (C) as shown.
  1. Remove the shift lever housing (D).
  1. Install the select cable (A) and shift cable (B) to the shift lever housing (C) as shown.
    NOTE: Painted the reference mark (D) on the select cable.
  1. Install the shift lever housing mounting bolts.
  1. Install the instrument center lower cover.
  1. Install the instrument middle panel.
