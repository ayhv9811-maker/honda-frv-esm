Door Key Cylinder Switch Test
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector from the key cylinder switch.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 5 [10] and No. 6 [9] terminals when the door key cylinder switch is LOCK position.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 7 [8] and No. 6 [9] terminals when the door key cylinder switch is UNLOCK position.
