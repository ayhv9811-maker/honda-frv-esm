Engine Oil Pan Removal
  1. If the engine is out of the vehicle, go to Step 6 .
  1. Drain the engine oil.
  1. Remove the front tyres/wheels.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Disconnect the primary heated oxygen sensor (primary HO2S) connector and secondary heated oxygen sensor (secondary HO2S) connector, then remove exhaust pipe A/three way catalytic converter (TWC) assembly.
  1. Remove the front stiffener (A) and rear stiffener (B), then remove the clutch cover (C).
  1. Remove the bolts/nuts securing the oil pan.
  1. Using a flat blade screwdriver, separate the oil pan from the block in the places shown.
  1. Remove the oil pan.
