Front Side Seat Disassembly/Reassembly - Without Leather
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
Seat-back
  1. Remove the front side seat.
  1. Remove the headrest.
  1. Driver's: Remove the manual hight handle (A).
    -1.
    Release the tabs (B) with a handy remover, and release the hook (C), then remove the cap (D).
    -2.
    Remove the bolts, then remove the handle.
  1. Remove the recline cover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the recline knob (B).
    -2.
    Remove the screws (C).
    -3.
    Detach the clips (D), and release the hooks (E), then remove the cover.
Driver's
Passenger's
  1. Remove the center cover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the screw (B).
    -2.
    Detach the clips (C), and release the hook (D), then remove the cover.
    NOTE: RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. From under the seat cushion, detach the side airbag connector (A), release the wire ties (B), and if equipped, disconnect and detach the seat-back heater connector (C). Release the clips (D) and pull the seat cushion cover (E) back.
  1. Fold the seat-back forward.
Driver's
Passenger's
  1. With side airbag: Release the hooks (A), and unzip the seat-back cover (B). RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Pull the side airbag harness (C) and if equipped, the seat-back heater harness (D) out through the holes (E) in the seat cushion cover (F).
  1. Turn over the seat-back cover (A). Release the clips (B), and detach the clips (C).
  1. Release the front hook (A) and the rear hook (B) of the reinforcing cloth (C) from the seat-back frame (D).
  1. Pull the side airbag harness (A) and if equipped, the seat-back heater harness (B) out through the holes (C) in the seat-back cover (D).
  1. Pull out the headrest guides (A) while pinching the end of the guides, and remove them.
  1. Remove the seat-back cover/pad (A) from the seat-back frame (B).
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat-back cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the clips, hooks, and hook strips.
    • Replace any clips you removed with new ones (A). Install them with commercially available upholstery ring pliers (B).
    • Reinstall the front hook (C) and rear hook (D) of the reinforcing cloth (E) securely.
    • Use only original Honda replacement seat-back covers.
    • Make sure the side airbag harness and seat-back heater harness, are routed properly.
Seat Cushion
  1. Remove the front side seat.
  1. Driver's: Remove the manual hight handle (A).
    -1.
    Release the tabs (B) with a handy remover, and release the hook (C), then remove the cap (D).
    -2.
    Remove the bolts, then remove the handle.
  1. Remove the recline cover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the recline knob (B).
    -2.
    Remove the screws (C).
    -3.
    Detach the clips (D), and release the hooks (E), then remove the cover.
Driver's
Passenger's
  1. Remove the center cover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the screw (B).
    -2.
    Detach the clips (C), and release the hook (D), then remove the cover.
    NOTE: RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. From under the seat cushion, detach the side airbag connector (A), release the wire ties (B), and if equipped, disconnect and detach the seat-back heater connector (C). Release the clips (D) and pull the seat cushion cover (E) back.
  1. Fold the seat-back forward.
Driver's
Passenger's
  1. Pull the side airbag harness (A) and if equipped, the seat-back heater harness (B) out through the holes (C) in the seat cushion cover (D). RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Release the hooks (A) from both sides the seat cushion.
  1. Release the hooks (A).
  1. Remove the seat cushion cover/pad (A) from the seat cushion frame (B), and the seat belt buckle (C) out of slit (D).
  1. Install the cushion in the reverse order of removal. To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat cushion cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hooks.
