Fuel Filter Replacement
The fuel filter should be replaced whenever the fuel pressure drops below the specified value, after marking sure that the fuel pump and the fuel pressure regulator are OK.
  1. Remove the fuel tank unit.
  1. Remove the fuel filter (A).
  1. Install the reverse order of removal with a new base gasket (B), fuel tank unit locknut (C), and new O-rings (D), then check these items.
    • When connecting the wire harness, make sure the connection is secure and the terminal (E) is firmly locked into place.
    • When installing the fuel gauge sending unit (F), make sure the connection is secure and the connector is firmly locked into place. Be careful not to bend or twist it excessively.
