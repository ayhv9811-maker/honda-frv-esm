Dashboard Center Middle Panel Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • Do not pry the panel surface with any tools to prevent damage.
  1. Without seat heater: Open the center pocket (A), and detach the clips by pulling the pocket out, then remove it.
  1. With seat heater: Detach the clips, and release the retaining tabs (A), then pull the seat heater switch panel (B) out. Disconnect the seat heater switch connectors (C) and detach the harness clip (D). RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Pull out along the lower edge of the dashboard center middle panel (A) by hand to release the lower clips (B, C) and the lower and side hooks (D).

RHD
  1. Pull out along the upper edge of the dashboard center middle panel (A) to detach the clips, then pull the panel back.
LHD
RHD
  1. Disconnect the climate control panel connector (A), and hazard warning switch connector (B), then remove the dashboard center middle panel (C).
  1. Install the panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure each connector is plugged in properly.
    • Replace any damaged clips with new ones.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
