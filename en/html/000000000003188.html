DTC Troubleshooting: U0155 (184)
DTC U0155:
 F-CAN Malfunction (ECM-Gauge assembly)
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0155 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Remove the gauge assembly.
  1. Disconnect the gauge assembly 28P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 2 (58P).
  1. Connect ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 47 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between gauge assembly 28P connector terminal No. 11 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the gauge assembly and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 47, then go to Step 12 .
  1. Connect ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 48 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between gauge assembly 28P connector terminal No. 27 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Substitute a known-good gauge assembly, then go to Step 13 and recheck. If DTC U0155 is not indicated, replace the original gauge assembly, then go to Step 13 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the gauge assembly and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 48, then go to Step 13 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0155 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
