Sunroof Inner Cable Replacement
  1. Remove the frame.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Drip rail
    • Sunroof motor
  1. Remove the screws, then remove both sunshade stoppers (A).
  1. To keep the sunshade (A) from getting dirtied, remove it from the guide rail (B).
  1. Remove the screw from under the guide rail (A).
  1. Pivot the culisse (A) down by sliding the slider (B) back.
  1. Slide the culisse (A) back with the drip link (B).
  1. Slide the culisse (A) back to remove the rear slider portion (B) of the drip link (C), and the slider (D) out of the guide rail (E).
  1. Remove the front slider portion (A) of the drip link (B) out of the guide rail (C) by sliding the culisse back.
  1. Remove the front slider portion (A) of the culisse (B) out of the guide rail (C), then separate the culisse with the slider (D), drip link (E), and the inner cable (F).
  1. Remove the inner cable from the guide rail.
  1. Slide the slider (A) forward fully, then remove it from the culisse (B).
  1. Remove the screws and release the hooks (A), then remove the drive unit assembly (B).
  1. Install the slider and cable in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Damaged parts should be replaced.
    • Apply multipurpose grease to the culisse (A) and guide rail portion of the frame (B) indicated by the arrows.
    • Apply liquid thread lock to the drive unit assembly mounting secrews before reinstallation.
    • Before reinstalling the motor, drip rail (C), sunshade (D), and sunshade stoppers (E), align both culisses (A):
      • -
      Install the screw (F) to the guide rail.
      -
      Slide both culisses rearward fully until rear end of the inner cable (G) contact with the screws.
      -
      T hen reinstall the motor.
      -
      Move both culisses forward by rotating the motor with the 4 mm hexagonal wrench, then reinstall the drip rail, sunshade, and sunshade stoppers.
    • Install the frame and glass, then check the opening drag.
    • After checking the opening drag, reinstall the motor, and perform the sunroof calibration.
