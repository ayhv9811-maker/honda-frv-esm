Radiator Fan High Speed Circuit Troubleshooting
  1. Remove the fan control relay from the auxiliary under-hood relay box, and test it.
Is the relay OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the fan control relay .■
  1. Measure the voltage between the fan control relay 5P socket terminal No. 5 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Check for open in the wire between fan control relay 5P socket terminal No. 5 and radiator fan diode 2P connector terminal No. 1.■
  1. Check for continuity between the fan control relay 5P socket terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Check for open in the wire between fan control relay 5P socket terminal No. 3 and engine control module (ECM)/powertrain control module (PCM) connector terminal A5.■
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the fan control relay 5P socket terminal No. 2 and body ground. If the wire is OK, check for poor ground at G301.■
