Brake Booster Replacement
  1. Remove the battery hold-down bracket, then remove the battery and battery tray.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the master cylinder.
  1. Disconnect the vaccum hose (A) from the brake booster (B).
  1. Remove the fuel filter (A), then move it aside.
  1. For RHD type: remove these items:
    • APP senser.
    • Hand primer bracket.
    • Brake booster vacuum hose holder.
    • A/C line bracket (A).
  1. Remove the brake pedal support member (A).
  1. Remove the lock pin (A) and the joint pin (B), then disconnect the yoke (C) from the brake pedal.
  1. Remove the brake booster mounting nuts (D).
  1. Remove the yoke from brake booster rod (E).
  1. For RHD type: move the brake booster toward the passenger side.
    -1.
    Move the brake booster toward the front.
    -2.
    Turn the brake booster until the brake booster rod comes downword.
    -3.
    Move the brake booster toward the passenger side with passing through the brake booster rod between engine and intake manifold.
    • Be careful not to damage the booster surfaces and threads of the brake booster rod and the booster stud bolts.
    • Be careful not to bend or damage the brake lines.
  1. Turn the brake booster (A) until the brake booster rod comes downward.
  1. Remove the brake booster (A) from the engine compartment.
  1. Install the brake booster in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Adjust the pushrod clearance before installing the brake booster.
    • Tighten all mounting hardware to the specified torque values.
    • Use a new lock pin whenever installing.
    • After installing the brake booster and master cylinder, fill the reservoir with new brake fluid from an unopened container bleed the brake system, and adjust the brake pedal height and free play.
