Tailgate Handle Replacement
NOTE: Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Tailgate lower trim panel
    • Rear license trim
  1. Disconnect the handle cable (A) from the tailgate handle (B).
  1. Remove the nuts.
  1. From outside of the tailgate, pull out the tailgate handle (A), then remove it.
  1. Install the tailgate handle in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the handle cable is connected securely.
    • Make sure the tailgate opens properly.
