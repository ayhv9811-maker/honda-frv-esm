Drive Belt Auto-tensioner Removal/Installation
  1. Loosen the water pump pulley mounting bolts.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Remove the alternator.
  1. Remove the water pump pulley.
  1. Remove the drive belt auto-tensioner (A).
  1. Check for oil leakage from the auto-tensioner dumper and check for damaged to the dumper rubber (B). If the damage or oil leaks, replace the auto-tensioner.
  1. Install the drive belt auto-tensioner, then do the auto-tensioner air bleeding.
  1. Install the water pump pulley.
  1. Install the alternator.
  1. Install the drive belt.
