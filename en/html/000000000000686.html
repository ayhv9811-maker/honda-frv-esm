M/T Countershaft Inspection
  1. Inspect the gear and bearing surfaces for wear and damage, then measure the countershaft at points A, B, and C. If any part of the countershaft is less than the service limit, replace it.

    Standard:

    A Needle Bearing Contact Area (Clutch Housing Side):
    33.000−33.015 mm (1.2992−1.2998 in.)
    B 1st Gear Contact Area:
    33.984−34.000 mm (1.3380−1.3386 in.)
    C Ball Bearing Contact Area (Transmission Housing Side):
    24.987−25.000 mm (0.9837−0.9843 in.)
    Service Limit:
    A:
    32.95 mm (1.297 in.)
    B:
    33.93 mm (1.336 in.)
    C:
    24.94 mm (0.982 in.)


  1. Inspect the runout by supporting both ends of the countershaft. Then rotate the countershaft two complete turns while measuring with a dial gauge. If the runout exceeds the service limit, replace the countershaft.
    Standard:
    0.02 mm (0.001 in.) max.
    Service Limit:
    0.05 mm (0.002 in.)
