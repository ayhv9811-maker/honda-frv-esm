Rear Window Wiper Motor Test
  1. Remove the tailgate trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the 4P connector (A) from the rear window wiper motor (B).
  1. Test the motor by connecting battery power to the wiper motor 4P connector No. 1 terminal and grounding the No. 3 terminal. The motor should run.
  1. If the motor does not run or fails to run smoothly, replace the motor.
