Side Defogger Vent Trim Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Right vent trim is shown; Left vent trim is symmetrical.
  1. Carefully insert a handy remover next to the front hook (A), and detach the front and upper hooks (A, B) by prying on along the upper edge of the side defogger vent trim (C).
  1. From the front to the rear, release the lower hooks (D) by pulling the vent trim up, then remove the vent trim.
  1. Insert the rear lower hook into the dashboard first, next insert other lower hooks, then push the upper and front hooks into place securely.
