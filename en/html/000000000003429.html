Substitute a known-good VSA modulator-control unit. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original VSA modulator-control unit. ■
  1. Disconnect VSA modulator-control unit 46P connector.
  1. Reconnect the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector.
  1. Measure resistance between ECM/PCM connector terminals A36 and A37.
Is there about 2.34−2.86 kΩ?
YES -
Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM/PCM, replace the original ECM/PCM.
NO -
Substitute a known-good yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor. If the HDS identify the vehicle, replace the original yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor. ■
