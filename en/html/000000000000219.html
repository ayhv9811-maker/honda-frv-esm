Front Air Spoiler Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the clips (B, C) from under the front bumper (A).
  1. Pull the front air spoiler (D) out to release the hooks (E), then remove the spoiler.
  1. Install the spoiler in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
