Front Center Seat Removal/Installation
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to scratch the body or tear the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, and wait at least 3 minutes before removing the seat.
  1. Remove the front passenger's side seat.
  1. Slide the seat all the way backward, and remove the seat track end covers (A) from the front of the seat tracks.
  1. Slide the seat all the way forward, and remove the seat track end covers (A) from the back of the seat tracks.
  1. Slide the seat to the rear, about halfway so you can access the seat mounting bolts.
  1. Remove the bolts (A, B) securing the front center seat tracks (C). When removing the middle mounting bolts, turn over the carpet as needed.
  1. Remove the under storage box (A).
  1. Disconnect the seat belt tensioner connector (A).
  1. Remove the headrest.
  1. With the help of an assistant, carefully remove the front seat through the front passenger's door opening.
  1. Install the seat in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the connector is plugged in properly.
    • Tighten the bolts by hand first, then tighten them to specification with a torque wrench.
    • Reconnect the negative cable to the battery.
    • Set the clock.
    • Perform the power window control unit reset procedure. And on D17A engine model, do the ECM idle learn procedure.
