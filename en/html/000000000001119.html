ECT Sensor 2 Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 2 connector (A), then remove the ECT sensor 2 (B).
  1. Install the ECT sensor 2 with a new O-ring (C).
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Refill the radiator with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
