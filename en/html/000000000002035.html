Radiator and Fan Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the lower hose.
  1. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 2 connector (A), then remove the harness clamp (B).
  1. Remove the bulkhead cover.
  1. Disconnect the fan motor connectors (A) and compressor clutch connector (B), then remove the harness clamps (C).
  1. Remove the nuts (A) securing the intake air duct.
  1. Remove the A/C condenser bracket (B) and radiator upper bracket (C), then remove the bolts (D) securing the bulkhead.
  1. Remove the hood latch (A), hood latch cable clamps (B), and harness clamps (C), then remove the bulkhead (D).
  1. Remove the upper radiator hose.
  1. Remove the reserve tank.
  1. Pull up the radiator.
  1. Remove the fan shroud assemblies and other parts from the radiator.
  1. Install the radiator in the reverse order of removal. Make sure the upper and lower cushions are set securely.
  1. Install the bulkhead in the reverse order of removal. Apply body paint to the bulkhead mounting bolts.
  1. Fill the radiator with engine coolant and bleed the air.
