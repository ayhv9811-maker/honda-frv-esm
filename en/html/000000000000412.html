Door Lock Actuator Test
Driver's Door Lock Actuator
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector (A) from the actuator.
  1. Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table. To prevent damage to the actuator, apply battery voltage only momentarily.
  1. If the actuator does not work as specified, replace it.
Driver's Super Locking Actuator
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector (A) from the actuator.
  1. Connect power to the No. 4 terminal and ground to the No. 3 terminals momentarily. The actuator should operate.
  1. If the actuator does not operate as specified, replace it.
Passenger's/Rear Door Lock Actuator
  1. Remove the passenger's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector (A) from the actuator.
  1. Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table. To prevent damage to the actuator, apply battery voltage only momentarily.
  1. If the actuator does not work as specified, replace it.
Passenger's/Rear Super Locking Actuator
  1. Remove the passenger's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector (A) from the actuator.
  1. Connect power to the No. 2 [4] terminal and ground to the No. 1 [3] terminals momentarily. The actuator should operate.
    [ ]:
    Right rear door

  1. If the actuator does not operate as specified, replace it.
