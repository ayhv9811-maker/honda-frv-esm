Rear Side Seat Cushion Disassembly/Reassembly
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the rear side seat.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the dampers (A).
  1. Release the fastener (A) and rear hooks (B), and then unzip the seat-back cover (C).
  1. Pull back the edge of the seat cushion cover (A) all the way around, and release the clips (B) from the seat cushion frame (C).
  1. Remove the seat cushion cover/pad (A) and the silencer (B) from the seat cushion frame (C).
  1. Reassemble the seat cushion in the reverse order of disassembly, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles in the seat cushion cover, stretch the material evenly over the pad.
    • Replace the clips you removed with new ones using commercially available upholstery ring pliers.
