Door Molding Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the door.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Using a utility knife, carefully cut the double-sided adhesive tape (A) along the edge of the door moldings (B).
Front door
Rear door
  1. Release all the clips by pushing their hooks with a scale (B) wrapped with protective tape while pulling the door molding (C) back, then remove the molding.
  1. Replace any damaged clips.
  1. Scrape off the remaining double-sided adhesive tape from the molding and door, then clean the molding and door surfaces with a sponge dampened in alcohol.
Front door
Rear door
  1. Attach the double-sided adhesive tape (A) to the molding (B), and fold the edge of each adhesive backing from the double-sided adhesive tape.
    Adhesive tape:
    Thickness 1.2 mm (0.047 in.)
    Width 5 mm (0.2 in.)

Front door
Rear door
  1. Hold the molding (A) up, and fit all the clips into the holes in the door, then push on the molding until the clips snap into place.
  1. Carefully pull the adhesive backing (B) away, and push the molding into place securely.
