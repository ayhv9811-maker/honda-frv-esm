A/T Assembly Removal
Special Tools Required

Engine support hanger
Â AAR-T1256-J00
This special tool is available from Snap-on Tools International, LLC.
NOTE:
  • Use fender covers to avoid damaging painted surfaces.
  • Engine support hanger (AAR-T1256-J00) must be used with the side engine mount installed.
  1. Remove the windshield wiper arms.
  1. Remove the cowl cover.
  1. Remove the front bulkhead cover.
  1. Make sure the ignition switch is OFF. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the battery hold-down bracket, and remove the battery cover, battery, and battery tray.
  1. Remove the intake air duct and air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the battery base and resonator.
  1. Raise the vehicle, and make sure it is securely supported.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the drain plug (A), and drain the automatic transmission fluid (ATF).
  1. Reinstall the drain plug with a new sealing washer (B).
  1. Fix the hood in the vertical position.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing bracket.
  1. Disconnect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A connector.
  1. Remove the bolts (B) securing the harness clamp brackets.
  1. Disconnect the connector from the 2nd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (A), and remove the bolts securing the harness clamp brackets (B).
  1. Disconnect the transmission range switch connector (A), and remove the connector from its bracket.
  1. Disconnect the output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor connector (B) and input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector (C).
  1. Disconnect the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector.
  1. Disconnect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C connector (A), A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve B connector (B), and shift solenoid harness connector (C), then remove the harness clamps (D) from the clamp brackets (E).
  1. Disconnect the ATF cooler hoses (A) from the ATF cooler lines (B). Turn the end of the ATF cooler hoses up to prevent ATF from flowing out, then plug the hoses and lines.
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A), remove the ACG terminal (B), and remove the harness clamp (C) from its bracket (D).
  1. Remove the bolts securing the harness covers on the engine.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A) and vacuum tube (B).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing bracket (C).
  1. Remove the bolts securing the harness cover (D), and remove the harness cover from its bracket.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the connector bracket (E).
  1. Remove the harness cover bracket (A), and install the hanger plate (B) to the bolt hole (C) of the harness cover bracket with 8 x 1.25-16 mm bolt (D).
  1. Install the engine support hanger (AAR-T1256-J00) to the vehicle, and attach the hook (A) to the hanger plate chain (B). Tighten the wing nut (C) by hand, and lift and support the engine.
  1. Disconnect the headlight adjuster leveling sensor connector (if equipped).
  1. Insert a 5 mm Allen wrench (A) in the top of the ball joint pin (B), and loosen the nut (C). Remove both nuts, and separate the stabilizer link (D) from the lower arms (E).
  1. Remove the spring clips (F) and ball joint nuts (G), and separate the knuckles (H) from the lower arms.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the ATF cooler line on the front subframe.
  1. Remove the lower torque rod bolts.
  1. Make the appropriate reference lines at positions A and B that line up with the center of the subframe mounting bolts (C).
  1. Support the front subframe with a wood piece and a jack.
  1. Remove the four bolts securing the front subframe, and lower the subframe.
  1. Remove the shift cable cover (A).
  1. Remove the bolts securing the shift cable brackets (B) (C).
  1. Remove the control lever (D) from the control shaft (E). Do not bend the shift cable excessively.
  1. Remove the shift cable cover bracket from the transmission.
  1. Remove the torque converter cover (A), and remove the drive plate bolts (B) (8) while rotating the crankshaft pulley.
  1. Remove the driveshafts from the differential. Coat all precision machined surfaces with clean engine oil, then put plastic bags over driveshaft ends.
  1. Remove the upper transmission housing mounting bolts.
  1. Place a jack under the transmission, raise it just enough to take off of the mount, and remove the transmission mount bracket bolts.
  1. Remove the front transmission housing mounting bolts.
  1. Remove the rear transmission housing mounting bolt.
  1. Remove the lower transmission housing mounting bolt (A), and pull out the bolt (B) to the limit of travel.
  1. Lower the transmission by loosening the wing nut of the engine support hanger, and tilt the engine just enough for the transmission to clear the side frame.
  1. Slide the transmission away from the engine to remove it from the vehicle, and lower the transmission on the jack.
  1. Remove the torque converter and dowel pins.
  1. Inspect the drive plate, and replace it if it's damaged.
