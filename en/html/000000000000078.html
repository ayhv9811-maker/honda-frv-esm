Steering Column/Lock Lever Inspection/Adjustment
  • Check the steering column ball bearing (A) and the steering joint bearings (B) for play and proper movement. If any bearing is noisy or has excessive play, replace the steering column as an assembly.
  • Check the pivot pin (C) for damage. If it is damaged, replace the steering column as an assembly.
  • Check the sliding plates (D) for distortion or breakage. If there is distortion or breakage, replace the steering column as an assembly.
  • Check the tilt mechanism and telescopic mechanism for movement.
  1. Move the tilt lever (A) from the loose position to the lock position three to five times. With the lock lever in the loose position measure the lock lever preload 10 mm (0.39 in.) from the end of the lock lever.
    Preload: 90 N (9.2 kgf, 20 lbf) Min.
  1. If the measurement is out of the specification, replace the steering column as an assembly.
