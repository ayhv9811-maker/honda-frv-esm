A/T Interlock System - Park Pin Switch Test
LHD Model
  1. Remove the following parts:
    • Dashboard center middle panel
    • Driver's middle panel
    • Center lower panel
  1. Disconnect the D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector.
  1. Shift the shift lever into the P position, and check for continuity between D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector terminals No. 2 and No. 6.
    There should be no continuity.
  1. Shift out of the P position, and check for continuity between connector terminals No. 2 and No. 6.
    There should be continuity.
  1. If the park pin switch tests OK, the test is finished.
    If the park pin switch fails the test, replace the park pin switch.
  1. Install the following parts:
    • Center lower panel
    • Driver's middle panel
    • Dashboard center middle panel
