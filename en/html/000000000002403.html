Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Trunk Area: Supplement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
Rear Floor Lid
  1. Fold the rear center seat and both rear side seats forward.
  1. Pull back the rear center seat-back cover (A) to detach the clips from the center floor lid assembly (B).
  1. Remove the center floor lid assembly (A).
    -1.
    Detach the clip (B).
    -2.
    Detach the clips (C) from the frame pipe (D) of the rear center seat (E).
    -3.
    Pull the center floor lid assembly out from between the bracket (F) and base of the rear floor lid assembly (G).
  1. Detach the clips (H) from both rear side seats (I).
  1. Reverse the rear floor lid assembly, detach the clips (J) from both trunk side trim panels (K), release the fasteners (L) from the right trunk spacer (M), left trunk spacer (N), and both trunk side trim panels, then remove the rear floor lid assembly.
  1. Remove both trunk spacers.
  1. Install the lid in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
