Dashboard Driver's Trim Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Tilt the steering column down.
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard trim (A).
    -1.
    Gently pull out along the upper edge to release the clips (B) and hooks (C).
    -2.
    Gently pull out along the edge of the panel to release the remaining clips (B, D, E, F).
  1. Install the panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips with new ones.
    • Push the clips and hooks into place securely.
