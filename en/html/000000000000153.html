Audio System Symptom Troubleshooting - Radio preset memory is lost
Radio preset memory is lost
  1. Inspect the under-dash No. 24 (15 A) fuse.
Is it OK?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Replace the fuse and reset. If it fails again, repair the short to ground in the harness.■
  1. Remove the audio unit.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between the audio unit connector A10 terminal and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
An open wire between the under-dash fuse/relay box and audio unit A10 terminal.■
  1. Check for continuity between the audio unit A20 terminal and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Faulty audio unit.■
NO -
An open wire between the audio unit A20 terminal and body ground (G402).■
  1. Operate 1 of 6 preset switches to see the symptom appear.
Does the symptom appear?
YES -
Faulty audio unit.■
NO -
Operation is normal.■
