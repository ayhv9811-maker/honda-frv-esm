Steering Column Tilt Operation Check
Check of Tilting Load
  1. Set the steering wheel in the straight driving position and loosen the lock lever fully.
  1. Attach the spring scale to the highest point of the steering wheel, and set the tilting position at the lowest.
  1. Pull the spring scale straight up and read the operation load during tilting.
  1. Attach the spring scale to the lowest point of the steering wheel.
  1. Pull the spring scale straight down and read the operation load during tilting.
    TILTING LOAD (UPWARD/DOWNWARD):
    Standard:
    70 N (7.1 kgf, 16 lbf) Max.

  1. If the measurement is larger than the specification, remove the steering column and check or adjust the lock lever preload.
