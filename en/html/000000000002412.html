Rear Side Seat-back Cover Replacement - With Leather
Leather Seat-back Cover
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the seat-back.
  1. Remove the headrest.
  1. Detach the clip, then remove the lock knob cover (A).
  1. Remove the screw, then remove the lock knob (A).
  1. Release the clips (A) and detach the clips (B), then remove the latch cover (C).
  1. Release the hooks (A), then remove the tether anchor cover (B).
  1. From behind the seat-back, detach the clips (A), and unzip the seat-back cover (B).
  1. Pull back the seat-back cover (A), then release the hook strips (B) and clips (C) from the seat-back frame (D).
  1. Pull back the edge of the seat-back cover (A) all the way around, and release the hook strips (B) from the seat-back frame.
  1. Pull out the seat-back pad (A) from the frame (B), then pull out the headrest guides (C) while pinching the end of the guides, and remove them.
  1. Remove the seat-back cover/pad (A) and the silencer (B) from the seat-back frame (C).
  1. Pull back the edge of the seat-back cover all the way around, and release the clips (A) and fasteners (B), then separate the seat-back cover (C) and pad.
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat-back cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hooks, fasteners, and clips.
    • Make sure the seat-back locks and unlocks properly.
    • Using commercially available upholstery ring pliers (A), replace all of the clips you removed with new ones (B).
