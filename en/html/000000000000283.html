Lights-on, Key in, Seat Belt Reminder, and Parking Brake Reminder Systems Circuit (Gauge Assembly) Input Test
  1. Remove the gauge assembly.
  1. Disconnect the gauge assembly connector A (20P) and B (28P) from the gauge assembly.
  1. Inspect the connector and socket terminals to be sure they are all making good contact.
    • If the terminals are bent, loose or corroded, repair them as necessary, and recheck the system.
    • If the terminals looks OK, go to Step 4 .
  1. With the connectors still disconnected, make these input tests at the connector.
    • If any test indicates a problem, find and correct the cause, then recheck the system.
    • If all the input tests prove OK, replace the gauge assembly.
