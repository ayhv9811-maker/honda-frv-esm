Side Sill Protection Tape Replacement
  1. Slowly peel up the old side sill protection tape.
  1. Clean the body bonding surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol. After cleaning, keep oil, grease, and water from getting on the surface.
  1. Peel the adhesive backing from the side sill protection tape.
  1. Align the alignment marks (A) of the side sill protection tape (B) with the body line (C), and align the edge of the apprication tape (D) with the rear door sill outer trim (E), then press the side sill protection tape into place.
