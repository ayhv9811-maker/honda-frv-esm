A/T Shift Cable Replacement
NOTE: The illustration shows the RHD model; LHD is similar.
  1. Remove the following parts:
    • Dashboard center middle panel
    • Driver's middle panel
    • Center lower panel
    • Dashboard center lower cover
  1. Lift the vehicle up on a lift, or apply the parking brake, block rear wheels, and raise the front of the vehicle. Make sure it is securely supported.
  1. Shift the shift lever into the R position.
  1. Slide the lock tab (A) down on the shift cable end holder (B).
  1. Grasp the shift cable lock (C) in the middle with angle-jaw needle-nose pliers (D), and remove it from the shift cable end and shift cable end holder. Do not pry the shift cable lock with a screwdriver, it may damage the shift cable end holder.
  1. Separate the shift cable end from the shift cable end holder.
  1. Rotate the socket holder (A) on the shift cable (B) a quarter turn; the tab (C) on the socket holder will be in the opening (D) of the socket holder bracket (E). Then slide the holder to remove the shift cable from the socket holder bracket.
  1. Remove the shift cable cover (A).
  1. Remove the bolts securing the shift cable brackets (B) (C).
  1. Remove the control lever (D) from the control shaft (E).
  1. Remove the nuts securing the shift cable grommet (A).
  1. Remove the shift cable grommet, and pull out the shift cable (B).
  1. Insert the new shift cable through the grommet hole (C), and install the grommet in its hole. Do not bend the shift cable excessively.
  1. Secure the shift cable grommet with the nuts.
  1. Install the control lever (A) over the control shaft (B).
  1. Secure the shift cable brackets (C) (D) with the bolts.
  1. Secure the control lever with the new lock washer (E) and the lock bolt (F), then bend the lock tab of the lock washer against the bolt head.
  1. Install the shift cable cover (G).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and verify that the R position indicator comes on at the gauge assembly.
  1. If necessary, push the shift cable (A) until it stops, and release it. Pull the shift cable back one step so that the shift cable is in R. Do not hold the shift cable guide (B) to adjust the shift position.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Make sure the shift lever is in R, and insert a 6.0 mm (0.24 in.) pin (A) into the positioning hole (B) on the shift lever bracket base and through the positioning hole on the shift lever. The shift lever is secured in the R position.
  1. Rotate the socket holder (A) on the shift cable (B) to place the tab (C) on the holder opposite the opening (D) in the socket holder bracket (E). Align the holder with the opening in the bracket, then slide the holder into the bracket. Rotate the holder a quarter turn until the holder lock (F) stops to secure the shift cable. Do not install the shift cable by twisting the shift cable guide (G).
  1. Install the shift cable (A) in the shift cable end holder (B). Keep the shift cable end and end holder free of grease.
  1. Install the new shift cable lock (A) to secure the shift cable end and shift cable end holder, then push the lock tab (B) up until it stops to lock the joint.
  1. Remove the 6.0 mm (0.24 in.) pin that was installed to hold the shift lever.
  1. Set the parking brake. Start the engine, and move the shift lever through all positions three times.
  1. Check the shift lever operation, A/T gear position indicator operation, and shift lock operation.
  1. Install the following parts:
    • Dashboard center lower cover
    • Center lower panel
    • Driver's middle panel
    • Dashboard center middle panel
