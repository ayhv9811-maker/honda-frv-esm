Transmission Mount Replacement
  1. Loosen the upper torque rod mounting bolt (A).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Remove the engine control module (ECM)/powertrain control module (PCM) cover, then remove the three bolts securing the ECM/PCM.
  1. Remove the ECM/PCM bracket.
  1. Support the transmission with a jack and wood block under the transmission.
  1. Remove the transmission mount stiffener (A), then remove the transmission mount (B).
  1. Install the transmission mount with the new mounting bolts (C), then install the transmission mount stiffener.
  1. Loosely tighten the new bolt and nuts (D).
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
M/T
A/T
  1. Loosen the lower torque rod mounting bolt (A).
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Tighten the transmission mounting bolt and nuts.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
M/T
A/T
  1. Tighten the lower torque rod mounting bolt.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Tighten the upper torque rod mounting bolt.
  1. Install the ECM/PCM bracket.
  1. Install the ECM/PCM, then install the ECM/PCM cover.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing assembly.
