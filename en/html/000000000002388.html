DTC Troubleshooting: P0602 (196-1)
DTC P0602:
 ECM Programming Error
NOTE:
  • When ECM update is not completed, DTC P0602 is stored.
  • Record all freeze data and review General Troubleshooting Information before you troubleshoot.
  1. Do the ECM updating.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0602 indicated?
YES -
Substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM, replace the original ECM. ■
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
