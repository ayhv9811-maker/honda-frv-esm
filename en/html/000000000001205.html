Fuel Tank Replacement
  1. Drain the fuel tank.
  1. Jack up the vehicle, and support it with jackstands.
  1. Remove the cover (A). Disconnect the hoses (B). Slide back the clamps, then twist the hoses as you pull to avoid damaging them.
  1. Place a jack or other support under the tank (C).
  1. Remove the strap bolts, and let the strap (D) hang free.
  1. Remove the fuel tank. If it sticks to the undercoat on its mount, carefully pry it off the mount.
  1. Install the remaining parts in the reverse order of removal.
