Dashboard Driver's Lower Cover Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Removes the driver's pocket (A):
    -1.
    Open the driver's pocket, and prese it against the upper springs (B).
    -2.
    Remove the driver's pocket by pulling the bottom of it up.
  1. Remove the screw from the pocket opening.
  1. Pull out the driver's dashboard lower cover (A) vertically to the switch panel surface to detach the clips (B, C). If equipped, disconnect the VSA off switch connector (E) and headlight adjuster switch connector (F).
  1. Install the dashboard lower cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Make sure each connector is plugged in properly.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
