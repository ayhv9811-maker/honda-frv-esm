Carpet Replacement
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to damage, wrinkle or twist the carpet.
  • Be careful not to damage the dashboard or other interior trim pieces.
  • RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Front side seats, both sides
    • Front center seat
    • Rear side seat, both sides
    • Rear center seat
    • Kick panels, both sides
    • Front door sill inner trim, both sides
    • Rear door sill inner trim, both sides
    • Center pillar lower trim, both sides
  1. Detach the clips, then remove the footrest (A).
    RHD is shown; LHD is similar.
  1. Detach the clips, then remove the center footrest guide (A).
  1. Remove the dashboard center lower cover.
  1. Release the clips (A) from both sides of the dashboard center frame, and release the fastener (B) at front driver's side, then pull the carpet (C) out from under the dashboard.
  1. Detach the clips (D) under both front door sill portions of the carpet.
  1. Remove the front portion of the trunk side trim panel, on both sides.
  1. Release the clips (A) from the rear portion of the carpet (B), and detach the clips (C) under both rear door sill portions of the carpet. Pull out the edge of the carpet from under both rear side trim panels (D).
  1. Pull both front side seat belt (E) out through each slit (F) in the carpet.
  1. Remove the carpet.
  1. If necessary, remove the passenger's foot pad (A) by removing the clips.
  1. Install the carpet in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Take care not to damage, wrinkle or twist the carpet.
    • Make sure the seat harnesses are routed correctly.
    • Push the fasteners and clips into place securely.
    • Slip the carpet under both rear side trim panel properly.
