A/T Interlock System - Shift Lock Stop, Shift Lock Stop Cushion Replacement
NOTE: Refer to the Shift Lever Disassembly and Reassembly LHD model as needed during the following procedures.
LHD Model
  1. Remove the shift lever assembly.
  1. Remove the tape.
  1. Remove the harness band.
  1. Remove the shift lock solenoid (A) by expanding its locks (B). Remove the shift lock solenoid plunger (C) and plunger spring (D).
  1. If replacing the shift lock stop or shift lock stop cushion, remove the shift lock stop (E), and remove the shift lock stop cushion (F).
  1. Replace the shift lock solenoid.
  1. Apply silicone grease to the pin (G) on the shift lever bracket, and install the shift lock stop over the pin. Be sure to install the shift lock stop cushion on the stop.
  1. Install the shift lock solenoid plunger and plunger spring in the shift lock solenoid.
  1. Apply silicone grease to the tip of the shift lock stop, and install the shift lock solenoid by aligning the joint of the shift lock solenoid plunger with the tip of the shift lock stop.
  1. Route the harnesses along the harness guide (A), and tie the harnesses and guide with the new band (B).
  1. Tie the harnesses together into bundles with new tape (A).
  1. Secure the harnesses with the new bands (B) at the clamp guides (C).
  1. Install the shift lever assembly.
