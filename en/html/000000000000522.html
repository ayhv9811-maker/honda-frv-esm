DTC Troubleshooting: 33-81
DTC 33-81:
 Short to Power in Left Side Curtain Airbag Inflator
Special Tools Required

SRS inflator simulator
 07SAZ-TB4011A

SRS simulator lead E
 07XAZ-S1A0200

SRS simulator lead F
 07XAZ-SZ30100
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on, and is DTC 33-81 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, go to the DTC troubleshooting index for troubleshooting details.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the left side curtain airbag 2P connector from the right side wire harness (A).
  1. Connect the SRS inflator simulator (2 Ω connector) and simulator lead E to the right side wire harness.
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 33-81 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Short to power in the left side curtain airbag inflator; replace the left side curtain airbag. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the right side wire harness 4P connector (A) from the floor wire harness 4P connector (B).
  1. Connect the SRS inflator simulator (2 Ω connectors) and simulator lead F to the floor wire harness.
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 33-81 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Short to power in the right side wire harness; replace the right side wire harness.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the SRS unit connector C (28P) from the SRS unit. Do not disconnect simulator lead from the right side wire harness 2P connector.
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Disconnect the SRS inflator simulator from SRS simulator lead E.
  1. Check for voltage between each terminal of the SRS simulator lead E and body ground. There should be 0.5 V or less.
Is the voltage as specified?
YES -
Faulty SRS unit; replace the SRS unit. ■
NO -
Short to power in the right side wire harness; replace the right side wire harness.■
