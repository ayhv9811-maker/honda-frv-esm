A/T Differential Backlash Inspection
  1. Install the drive shaft and intermediate shaft into the differential assembly, and place the axles on the V-blocks.
  1. Measure the backlash of the pinion gears (A) with a dial indicator (B).
    Standard:
    0.05−0.15 mm (0.002−0.006 in.)

  1. If the backlash is out of standard, replace the differential carrier.
