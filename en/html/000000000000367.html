Front Side Seat Removal/Installation
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE
  • Take care not to scratch the body or tear the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, and wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Remove the front inner seat track end cover (A) and front outer seat track end cover (B) from the front of both seat tracks.
  1. Remove the rear outer seat track end cover (A) and rear inner seat track end cover (B) from the back of both seat tracks.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the front seat (A).
  1. Lift up the front seat, then detach the harness clips (A), and disconnect the seat belt switch connector (B) on the driver's seat, and the navigation unit connector (C) on the passenger's seat, and the seat heater connector (D) and the side airbag connector (E) on the driver's and passenger's seat.
  1. With the help of an assistant, carefully remove the front seat through the front door opening.
  1. Install the seat in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure each connector is plugged in properly.
    • Apply liquid thread lock to the front seat mounting bolts before reinstallation.
    • Tighten the seat mounting bolts to the specified torque in the sequence shown. Slide the seat all the way back and tighten (1) and (2), then slide it forward and tighten (3) and (4).
    • Reconnect the negative cable to the battery.
