Turbocharger and Exhaust Manifold Removal and Installation

Exploded View
Removal
  1. Disconnect the exhaust gas temperature (EGT) sensor 1 connector (A), and remove the harness clamps (B).
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the splash shield and engine under cover.
  1. Loosen the bands (A) securing the turbocharger outlet hose and intercooler intake hose, and remove the intercooler intake hose (B), then remove the intercooler intake pipe mounting bolts (C).
  1. Remove the intercooler intake pipe bracket (D), then remove the intercooler intake pipe (E).
  1. Remove the turbocharger outlet hose.
  1. Remove exhaust pipe A.
  1. Remove the warm-up catalytic converter bracket (A), then remove the warm-up catalytic converter (B).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the air flow tube.
  1. Remove the breather hose (A) and vacuum hoses (B), then remove the air flow pipe bracket (C).
  1. Remove the vacuum line (D) from the cylinder head cover.
  1. Remove the exhaust manifold cover.
  1. Remove the sealing bolt (A) and water bypass hose (B).
  1. Remove the turbocharger stay (A), then remove the oil bypass pipe (B).
  1. Remove the turbocharger assembly, then seal the air inlet, air outlet, oil line hole, and water line hole with tape.
    NOTE: Use care when handing the turbocharger assembly. Do not turn the actuator rod adjusting nut, or touch the impeller.
  1. Remove the water bypass pipe (A), breather pipe (B), and air flow pipe (C).
  1. Remove the cover (A), then remove the exhaust joint (B).
  1. Remove the exhaust manifold.
Installation
  1. Install the exhaust manifold (A) with a new gasket (B) and new self-locking nuts (C), and tighten the nuts in a crisscross pattern in two or three steps, beginning with the inner nut.
  1. Install the exhaust joint (A) with new gasket (B), then install the cover (C).
  1. Install the air flow pipe (A), water bypass pipe (B), and breather pipe (C) with new gaskets (D).
  1. Pour new engine oil into turbocharger oil line.
  1. Install the turbocharger assembly (A) with a new gasket (B).
    NOTE: Use care when installing the turbocharger assembly. Do not turn the actuator rod adjusting nut, or touch the impeller.
  1. Check the bolt (A) and oil bypass pipe (B) for clogged. If they are clogged clean or replace the bolt and/or pipe.
  1. Install the oil bypass pipe with new gaskets (C) and new washers (D).
  1. Install the turbocharger stay (E).
  1. Install the sealing bolt (A) with new washers (B).
  1. Install the water bypass hose (C).
  1. Install the exhaust manifold cover.
  1. Install the vacuum line (A) to the cylinder head cover.
  1. Install the air flow pipe bracket (B), then install breather hose (C) and vacuum hoses (D).
  1. Install the air flow tube.
  1. Install the engine cover.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the warm-up catalytic converter (A). Use new gasket (B), then install warm-up catalytic converter bracket (C).
  1. Install exhaust pipe A. Use new gaskets (B) and new self-locking nuts (C).
    NOTE: Tighten the self-locking nuts (C) sequentially.
  1. Install the turbocharger outlet hose, and align the mark (A) on the hose with the pointer (B) on the turbocharger, then secure the hose with the band.
  1. Install the intercooler intake pipe (A) to the turbocharger outlet hose (B), then install the intercooler intake pipe bracket (C).
  1. Loosely tighten the intercooler intake pipe mounting bolts (D). Align the mark (E) on the intercooler intake pipe and the mark (F) on the turbocharger outlet hose, and slide the turbocharger outlet hose over the intercooler intake pipe until the hose end contact the bulge (G).
  1. Tighten the intercooler intake pipe mounting bolts, and secure the turbocharger outlet hose with the band (H), then install the intercooler intake hose (I), and secure the hose with band (J).
  1. Connect the exhaust gas temperature (EGT) sensor 1 connector (A), and install the harness clamps (B).
  1. Refill the expansion tank with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
  1. Install the splash shield and engine under cover.
