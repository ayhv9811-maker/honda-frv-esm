Cylinder Head Cover Removal
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the four ignition coils.
  1. Remove the throttle cable clamps (A), harness holder mounting bolts (B), dipstick (C), and harness clamp (D).
  1. Disconnect the alternator connector (A), BLK wire (B), and harness clamp (C) from the alternator.
  1. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor connector and exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve connector.
  1. Remove the power steering (P/S) hose from its clamp.
  1. Remove the breather hose, then remove the cylinder head cover.
