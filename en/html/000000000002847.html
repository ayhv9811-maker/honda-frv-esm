ATF Cooler Hose Replacement
  1. Slide the ATF cooler hose (A) on the ATF filter (B) until the hose end contacts the filter housing, and secure the hose with the clip (C) at 6−8 mm (0.24−0.31 in.) (D) from the filter housing. Install the cooler hose on the other side of the ATF filter in the same manner.
  1. Slide the ATF cooler hoses (E) over the ATF lines (F) until the hose ends contact the bulge (G), and secure the hoses with the clip (H) at 6−8 mm (0.24−0.31 in.) (I) from the hose end.
  1. Slide the ATF cooler hose (J) over the ATF cooler line (K) until the hose end contacts the bulge (L) with the white dot (M) facing up, and secure the hose with the clip (N) at 6−8 mm (0.24−0.31 in.) (O) from the hose end.
  1. Slide the ATF cooler hose (P) over the ATF line (Q) until the hose end contacts the line bracket (R), and secure the hose with the clip (S) at 6−8 mm (0.24−0.31 in.) (T) from the hose end.
  1. Slide the ATF cooler hoses (U) over the ATF cooler lines (V) until the hose ends contact the bulge (W), and secure the hoses with the clip (X) at 2−4 mm (0.08−0.16 in.) (Y) from the hose end.
