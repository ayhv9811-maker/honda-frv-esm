  1. Remove the cable bracket (A), then disconnect the cables (B) from the top of the transmission housing. Carefully remove both cables and the bracket together to avoid bending the cables.

  1. Support the subframe with a 4 x 4 x 40 in. piece of wood and a jack.
  1. Remove the front suspension subframe.
  1. Remove the transmission mount bolt, nuts, bolt, and the ground cable bolt.

  1. Remove the intercooler pipe.
