DTC Troubleshooting: 53
DTC 53:
 Motor Stuck ON
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
Is the pump motor working?
YES -
Replace the ABS control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the ABS control unit 26P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between ABS control unit 26P connector terminal No. 26 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between ABS modulator-control unit and body ground (G303).■
  1. Reconnect the ABS control unit 26P connector.
  1. Clear the DTCs.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle at 15 km/h (9 mph) or more.
  1. Check the DTCs.
Does the ABS indicator come on, and are DTC 53 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections in the ABS control unit 26P connector. If connections are good, replace the ABS modulator-control unit .■
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
