Resonator Removal/Installation
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the audio system and the navigation system (if equipped).
  1. Remove the battery.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the battery base (A), and the air intake ducts (B).
  1. Remove the resonator (C).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Install the battery, then enter the anti-theft codes for the audio system and the navigation system (if equipped).
