Front Center Seat Rear Slide Knob Replacement
NOTE:
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Right recline cover
    • Left recline cover
    • Cushion rear trim
  1. Remove the rear slide knob (A).
    -1.
    Detach the slide cable (B) from the bracket (C).
    -2.
    Release the E clip (D), then remove the shaft (E).
    -3.
    Pull out the knob from the bracket, and remove the spring (F).
    -4.
    Disconnect the slide cable from the knob.
    -5.
    If necessary, remove the bushings (G) from the bracket.
  1. Install the knob in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the slide cable is connected securely.
    • Replace the E clip you removed with new one.
    • Check the seat slide operation.
