Blower Unit Removal and Installation
NOTE: RHD type is shown, LHD type is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the passenger's dashboard under cover and the glove box.
  1. Remove the bolts. Cut the plastic cross brace (A) in the glove box opening with diagonal cutters in the area shown.
  1. Remove the ECM, D17A2 engine model, K20A9 engine model.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A) from the blower motor and the power transistor.
  1. Disconnect the connector (A) from the recirculation control motor. Remove the mounting nuts, the mounting bolts and the blower unit (B).
  1. Install the unit in the reverse order of removal. Make sure that there is no air leakage.
