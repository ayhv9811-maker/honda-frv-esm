Parking Brake Shoe Lining Break-in
NOTE: Do the brake linings surface brake-in when replacing shoes with new linings and/or new rear brake disc/drum.
Do this operation in a safe area.

  1. Park the vehicle on a firm, level surface.
  1. Do the major parking brake adjustment.
  1. Do the minor parking brake adjustment.
  1. Drive the vehicle at 50 km/h (31 mph).
  1. Pull the parking brake lever with 196 N (20 kgf, 44 lbf) of force while driving the vehicle for 100 m (1/16 mile).

  1. Check the parking brake lever adjustment.
