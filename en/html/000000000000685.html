M/T Countershaft Assembly Clearance Inspection
  1. Measure the clearance between the 1st gear (A) and the thrust washer (B) with a feeler gauge (C). If the clearance is more than the service limit, go to Step 2 .
    Standard:
    0.06−0.18 mm (0.002−0.007 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.23 mm (0.009 in.)

  1. Measure the thickness of the thrust washer.
    • If the thickness is not within the standard, replace thrust washer.
    • If the thickness is within the standard, go to Step 3 .
    Standard:
    1.95−1.97 mm (0.077−0.078 in.)

  1. Measure the thickness of the 1st gear.
    • If the thickness is not within the standard, replace the 1st gear.
    • If the thickness is within the standad, replace the 1st/2nd synchro hub.
    Standard:
    26.95−27.00 mm (1.061−1.063 in.)

  1. Measure the clearance between the 2nd gear (A) and 3rd gear (B) with a feeler gauge (C). If the clearance is more than the service limit, go to Step 5 .
    Standard:
    0.07−0.14 mm (0.003−0.006 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.18 mm (0.007 in.)

  1. Measure the thickness of the distance collar.
    • If the thickness is not within the standard, replace the distance collar.
    • If the thickness is within the standard, go to Step 6 .
    Standard:
    29.07−29.09 mm (1.144−1.145 in.)

  1. Measure the thickness of the 2nd gear.
    • If the thickness is not within the standard, replace 2nd gear.
    • If the thickness is within the standard, replace the 1st/2nd synchro hub.
    Standard:
    28.92−28.97 mm (1.139−1.141 in.)
