Tailgate Hinge Cover Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the tailgate hinge cover and body.
  1. Open the tailgate. Release the inside hook (A) from the tailgate hinge (B) by pulling the rear of the tailgate hinge cover (C) up.
  1. Slide the tailgate hinge cover (A) rearward to release it from the pin (B), and to disengage it from the rear end of the roof molding (C) (without roof rail), then move the cover forward while lifting it.
  1. Close the tailgate, then remove the tailgate hinge cover (A).
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal.
