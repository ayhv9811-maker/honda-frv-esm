Front Bulkhead Cover Replacement
NOTE:
  • When prying with a flat-tip screwdriver, wrap it with protective tape to prevent damage.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to scratch the front bulkhead cover and front grille.
  1. Remove the clips (A, B), and slide the bulkhead cover (C) to release the slits (D) of the cover from the front grill (E), then remove the front bulkhead cover.
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
