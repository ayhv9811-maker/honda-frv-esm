DTC Troubleshooting: P0563 (34)
DTC P0563:
 ECM Power Source Circuit Unexpected Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Wait 10 seconds.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0563 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the No. 12 +B IGP (15 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box, PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN) and at the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector E (31P).
  1. Measure voltage between ECM connector terminal E7 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Remove PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN) (A).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector terminal E7 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM (E7) and PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN), then go to Step 19 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Reconnect ECM connector E (31P).
  1. Measure voltage between ECM connector terminal E7 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
Go to Step 26 .
  1. Disconnect ECM connector A (31P).
  1. Measure voltage between body ground and ECM connector terminals A3 and A2 individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Go to Step 26 .
  1. Remove PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN) (A).
  1. Measure voltage between body ground and ECM connector terminals A3 and A2 individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the ECM (A2, A3) and PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN), then go to Step 19 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Replace PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN) (A).
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Wait 10 seconds.
  1. Do the ECM idle learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0563 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN) and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0563 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN) and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. ■
