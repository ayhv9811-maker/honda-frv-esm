Front Center Seat Turn Over Cushion Cover/Pad Replacement - Without Leather
NOTE:
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the turn over cushion.
  1. Detach the clips (A, B) by pulling the table board (C) out.
  1. Release the hook strips (A) and the clips (B), then pull back the cushion cover. And release the clip (C), then remove the strap (D) from the cushion wire (E).
  1. Remove the turn over cushion cover/pad (A) from the cushion frame (B).
  1. Install the cover/pad in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a turn over cushion cover/pad, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hook and clips.
    • Replace any clips you removed with new ones using commercially available upholstery ring pliers.
