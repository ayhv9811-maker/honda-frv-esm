Front Splash Shield Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove the splash shield (A).
    -1.
    From both wheel arch, remove the clips (B), securing the front inner fender (C) and splash shield (on the body).
    -2.
    From under the front bumper (D), remove the clips (E), and screws (F).
    -3.
    Release the hooks (G) of the splash shield, then pull the splash shield out.
  1. Install the splash shield in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clip.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
