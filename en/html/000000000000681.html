M/T Countershaft Bearing Replacement
Special Tools Required

Adjustable bearing remover set
 07JAC-PH80000

Driver
 07749-0010000

Attachment, 62 x 68 mm
 07746-0010500
  1. Remove the bearing set plate (A) from the clutch housing (B).
  1. Remove the needle bearing (A) using the special tool, then remove the oil guide plate C (B).
  1. Position the oil guide plate C (A) and new needle bearing (B) in the bore of the clutch housing (C).
  1. Install the bearing plate (A), and stake the bolt heads into the groove in the bearing set plate.
