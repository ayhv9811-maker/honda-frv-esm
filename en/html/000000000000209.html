Rear Door Latch Replacement
NOTE: Put on gloves to protect your hands.
Latch Replacement
  1. Remove these items:
    • Door panel
    • Panel bracket
  1. Disconnect the power door lock actuator connector and detach the harness clip.
  1. Remove the plastic cover.
  1. Raise the glass fully.
  1. Remove the latch mounting bolt and screws, then lower the latch.
  1. Remove the maintenance cap.
  1. Disconnect the outer handle rod.
  1. Remove the bolt securing the rear lower channel (A).
  1. Detach the latch cable (A) and inner handle cable (B) from the holder (C), then remove the latch (D) out between the rear lower channel (E) and the door. Take care not to bend the outer handle rod (F), latch cable, and inner handle cable.
  1. Install the latch in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the connector is plugged in properly, and each rod is connected securely.
    • Make sure the door locks and opens properly.
Cable Replacement
  1. Remove the latch.
  1. Remove the lever cover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the screw.
    -2.
    Release the hooks (B) from the latch body (C).
    -3.
    Remove the lever cover from under the latch (D) with its forward side away from the body. Take care not to damage the connector seal (E) with the cover.
  1. Disconnect the latch cable (A).
  1. Disconnect the inner handle cable (A).
  1. Install the cable in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure each cable is connected securely.
    • Make sure the door latches and unlatches properly.
    • While installing the lever cover, take care not to damage the connector seal.
