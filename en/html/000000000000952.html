Engine Oil Pan Installation
  1. Clean and dry the oil pan mating surfaces and bolt holes.
  1. Apply liquid gasket, P/N 08C70-K0334M or 08C70-X0331S, evenly to the oil pan mating surface of the block and to the inner threads of the bolt holes.
    NOTE:
    • Apply liquid gasket 4 mm wide.
    • Apply liquid gasket doubly to the jointing point of the liquid gasket.
  1. Install the oil pan.
  1. Tighten the bolts in two or three steps. In the final step, tighten all bolts, in sequence to 12 N·m (1.2 kgf·m, 8.7 lbf·ft). Wipe off the excess liquid gasket on the each side of crankshaft pulley and flywheel.
    NOTE: Wait at least 30 minutes before filling the engine with oil.
  1. Install the clutch cover (A), then install the front stiffener (B) and rear stiffener (C).
  1. If the engine is still in the vehicle, do the following steps.
  1. Install exhaust pipe A/three way catalytic converter (TWC) assembly. Use new gaskets and new self locking nuts, then connect the primary heated oxygen sensor (primary HO2S) connector and secondary heated oxygen sensor (secondary HO2S) connector.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Install the front tyres/wheels.
  1. Refill the engine with engine oil.
