Alternator Removal/Installation
Removal
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then write down the customer's audio presets.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the front grill cover.
  1. Remove the intake stay.
  1. Disconnect the hood switch connector (A), then remove the A/C condenser stay/rubber (B), radiator upper mount bracket/cushion (C), hood latch (D), left radiator upper mount bracket/cushion/rubber (E), and intercooler stay (F).
  1. Remove the bulkhead (A), then remove the harness clamps (B).
  1. Remove the water bypass hose (C).
  1. Disconnect the A/C condenser fan motor connector (A).
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the harness clamps (A), then disconnect the radiator fan switch (Low) connector (B), radiator fan motor connector (C), and A/C compressor clutch connector (D).
  1. Lower the vehicle on the hoist.
  1. Remove the two bolts, and loosen the two bolts securing the radiator fan shroud, then move the radiator fan shroud assembly to left side of the vehicle.
  1. Remove the A/C compressor clutch connector (A) and water bypass pipe (B) from the A/C condenser fan shroud.
  1. Remove the two bolts (C), and loosen the two bolts (D) securing the A/C condenser fan shroud, then remove the A/C condenser fan shroud assembly (E).
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Disconnect the alternator connector (A) and BLK wire (B), and remove the harness clamp (C) from the alternator.
  1. Remove the four bolts, then remove the alternator oil shield (A) and the alternator (B).
Installation
  1. Install the alternator (A) and the alternator oil shield (B).
  1. Connect the alternator connector (A) and BLK wire (B), and install the harness clamp (C) to the alternator.
  1. Install the A/C condenser fan shroud assembly (A).
  1. Install the A/C compressor clutch connector (B) and water bypass pipe (C) to the A/C condenser fan shroud.
  1. Install the radiator fan shroud.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Install the harness clamps (A), then connect the radiator fan switch (Low) connector (B), radiator fan motor connector (C), and A/C compressor clutch connector (D).
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the hoist.
  1. Connect the A/C condenser fan motor connector (A).
  1. Install the harness clamps (A), then install the bulkhead (B). Apply body paint to the bulkhead mounting bolt.
  1. Install the water bypass hose (C).
  1. Install the A/C condenser stay/rubber (A), radiator upper mount bracket/cushion (B), hood latch (C), left radiator upper mount bracket/cushion/rubber (D), and intercooler stay (E), then connect the hood switch connector (F).
  1. Install the intake stay.
  1. Adjust the hood latch alignment.
  1. Install the front grill cover.
  1. Connect the positive cable to the battery first, and then connect the negative cable.
  1. Enter the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
  1. Set the clock.
