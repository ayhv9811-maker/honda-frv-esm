Dashboard Driver's Middle Panel Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  1. Remove the dashboard center middle panel.
  1. Pull out the driver's dashboard inner panel (A) to detach the clips and hooks (B), then remove the panel.
  1. Install the inner panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clip and hook portions into place securely.
