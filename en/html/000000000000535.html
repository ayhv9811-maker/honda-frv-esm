DTC Troubleshooting: 42-C1
DTC 42-C1:
 Faulty Power Supply to the Right Front Impact Sensor
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on, and is DTC 42-C1 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, go to the DTC troubleshooting index.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the right engine compartment wire harness 2P connector (A) from the right front impact sensor.
  1. Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P) from the SRS unit.
  1. Check resistance between the No. 28 terminal of SRS unit connector A (28P) and body ground. There should be an open circuit or at least 1 MΩ.
Is the resistance as specified?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Short to ground in the dashboard wire harness or right engine compartment wire harness; replace the faulty harness.■
