  1. Remove the vehicle speed sensor (VSS) (A) and O-ring (B).

  1. Remove the drain plug (A), filler plug (B), and flange bolt (C).

  1. Remove the transmission hanger (B).
  1. Remove the 32 mm sealing cap (A).
  1. Expand the 56 mm snap ring (B) on the countershaft ball bearing, and remove it from the groove using a pair of snap ring pliers.
  1. Separate the transmission housing (C) from the clutch housing (D).
  1. Remove the 14 x 20 mm dowel pins (E).
  1. Remove the oil gutter plate (A), 78 mm shim (B), and oil guide plate M.
  1. Remove the select spring bolt.
  1. Remove the spring pin (A) using the special tool to a half, then pull out the spring pin using a nipper.
  1. Remove the select lever (A), select return spring (B), 5th reverse select return spring (C), 22 x 32 x 1 mm thrust washer (D), and the select arm (E).
  1. Remove the oil seal (F).
  1. Remove the reverse lock cam.
  1. If necessary, check the clearance of the reverse shift fork.
  1. Remove the reverse shift fork.
  1. Remove the reverse gear shaft (A) and the reverse idler gear (B).
  1. Apply tape to the mainshaft splines to protect the seal, then remove the mainshaft assembly (A) and countershaft assembly (B) with the shift forks (C) from the clutch housing (D).
