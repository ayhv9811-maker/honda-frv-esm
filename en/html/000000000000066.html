Brake System Indicator Circuit Troubleshooting - With VSA
Brake system indicator does not come on (check bulb operation with parking brake)
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and watch the brake system indicator.
Does the brake system indicator come on and then go off ?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Do the gauge assembly self-diagnostic function.
Is gauge assembly OK?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Replace the gauge assembly. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the VSA control unit 46P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Connect the VSA control unit 46P connector terminal No. 16 and body ground with a jumper wire.
Does the brake system indicator come on?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections in the VSA control unit 46P connector. If connections are good, replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the gauge assembly and the VSA modulator-control unit.■
Brake system indicator does not go off
If the brake system indicator does not go off with ABS indicators at the same time, do the ABS indicator circuit troubleshooting first, then recheck.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Release the parking brake.
Does the brake system indicator go off?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Check the brake fluid level.
Is the level OK?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Check the brake pad thickness and for brake fluid leakage. If they are OK, refill the brake fluid, and recheck.■

YES -

NO -

YES -

NO -
