M/T Mainshaft Disassembly
  1. Remove the angular ball bearing (A) and the tapered cone ring using a commercially available bearing separator (B) and bearing puller (C).
  1. Support 5th gear (A) on steel blocks, and press the mainshaft out of the 5th/6th synchro hub (B). Use of a jaw-type puller can damage the gear teeth.
  1. Support 3rd gear (A) on steel blocks, and press the mainshaft out of the 3rd/4th synchro hub (B). Use of a jaw-type puller can damage the gear teeth.
