Audio System Symptom Troubleshooting - Radio tuner does not change
Radio tuner does not change
  1. Check the audio-clock display.
Does the audio-clock display properly?
YES -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Check the audio-clock display.
Does it display?
YES -
Faulty display panel control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Operate the tuning switch to see if the audio-clock display LCD display changes.
Does it change?
YES -
Faulty audio unit.■
NO -
Operation is normal.■
