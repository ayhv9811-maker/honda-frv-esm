Speaker Replacement
Front Speaker
  1. Remove the door panel.
  1. Push down on the clip (A), then pull the top of the speaker (B) straight out just enough to release the upper clip. If you pull the speaker out too far, you will damage the lower clips (C). Then lift the speaker straight up to release the lower clips.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector, and remove the speaker.
  1. Install the speaker in the reverse order of removal.
Rear Speaker
  1. Remove the rear side trim panel.
  1. Remove the screws
  1. Remove the speaker (A), then disconnect the 2P connector (B).
  1. Install the speaker in the reverse order of removal.
Tweeter Speaker
  1. Remove the front pillar trim.
  1. Remove the screws, and remove the tweeter speaker (A) from the front pillar trim (B).
    NOTE: Take care not to scratch the dashboard.
