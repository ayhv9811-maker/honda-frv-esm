Radiator and Fan Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the front bulkhead cover.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the lower radiator hose (A).
  1. Remove the automatic transmission fluid (ATF) cooler hoses (B), then plug the hose and line.
  1. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 2 connector (A), and remove the harness clamp (B).
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Disconnect the fan motor connectors (A), then remove the harness clamps (B).
  1. Remove the bolt (A) securing the A/C line.
  1. Remove the A/C condenser brackets (B), then remove the bolts (C) securing the bulkhead.
  1. Remove the hood latch (A), hood latch cable clamps (B), and harness clamps (C), then remove the bulkhead (D).
  1. Remove the upper radiator hose.
  1. Remove the coolant reservoir.
  1. Pull up the radiator, then remove the radiator fan shroud assembly (A), A/C condenser fan shroud assembly (B), radiator cap (C), ETC sensor 2 (D), and drain plug (E).
  1. Reassemble the radiator with new O-rings (F).
  1. Install the radiator. Make sure the lower cushions (G) are set securely.
  1. Install the coolant reservoir.
  1. Install the upper radiator hose.
  1. Install the hood latch (A), hood latch cable clamps (B), and harness clamps (C) to the bulkhead (D).
  1. Install the bulkhead (A), then install the A/C condenser brackets (B).
  1. Install the A/C line (C).
  1. Apply body paint to the bulkhead mounting bolts.
  1. Connect the fan motor connectors (A), then install the harness clamps (B).
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Install the lower radiator hose (A) and ATF cooler hoses (B).
  1. Connect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 2 connector (A), and install the harness clamp (B).
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Install the front bulkhead cover.
  1. Refill the radiator with engine coolant, and bleed the air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
