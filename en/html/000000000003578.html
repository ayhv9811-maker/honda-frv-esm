Rear License Trim Replacement
NOTE: Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the tailgate lower trim panel.
  1. From inside the tailgate, remove the nuts securing the rear license trim (A).
  1. From inside the tailgate, release the clips while holding the rear license trim (A), then remove the trim. Take care not to scratch the tailgate.
  1. If necessary, remove the screw, then remove the lower license trim (A) from the upper license trim (B).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check if the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
