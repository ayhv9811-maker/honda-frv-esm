Rocker Arm Assembly Installation
  1. If the rocker arm assembly is disassembled, reassemble the rocker arm assembly.
  1. Install the oil control orifice (A) with a new O-ring (B), then install the rocker arm assembly (C).
  1. Install the lost motion (D) and the lost motion holder (E).
  1. Tighten each bolts two turns at a time in sequence.
    Specified Torque
    6 x 1.0 mm
    15 N·m (1.5 kgf·m, 11 lbf·ft)

  1. Adjust the valve clearance.
  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
