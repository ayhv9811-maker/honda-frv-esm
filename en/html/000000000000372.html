Front Center Seat Slide Cable Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to kink the cable.
  1. Remove the seat-back cover/pad, then remove the seat cushion cover/pad.
  1. Remove the retractor cover.
  1. Disconnect the slide cable (A) at both seat tracks (B), slide lever bracket (C), and rear slide knob (D), then remove the cable.
    -1.
    Release the push nut (E). Disconnect the cable from the slide lever, then detach the cable from the cable bracket (F).
    -2.
    Remove the bolt, then detach the cable from the cable bracket (G).
    -3.
    Detach the cable from the cable bracket (H), then disconnect it from the rear slide knob.
  1. Install the cable in the reverse order a removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the cable is connected securely.
    • Check the seat slide operation.
