Sunroof Wind Deflector Replacement
  1. Open the sunroof glass fully.
  1. Remove the screws securing the front stoppers (A) while holding them. Remove the front stoppers from both guide rails by sliding them forward.
  1. Remove the wind deflector (A).
  1. Remove the front stoppers (A) from the deflector springs (B) on both sides.
  1. Install the deflector in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Apply multipurpose grease to the holes in both guide rails where the deflector arms are installed.
    • Apply liquid thread lock to the mounting screws before reinstallation.
