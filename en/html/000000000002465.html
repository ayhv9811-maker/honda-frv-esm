Radiator Fan Switch (High) Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the lower radiator hose.
  1. Disconnect the radiator fan switch (High) connector (A), then remove the radiator fan switch (High) (B).
  1. Install the radiator fan switch (High) with a new O-ring (C).
  1. Install the lower radiator hose.
  1. Refill the expansion tank with engine coolant and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
