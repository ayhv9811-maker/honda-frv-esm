Fuel Fill Door Opener Replacement
  1. Remove these items:
    • Front side seat, driver side
    • Kick panel
    • Center pillar lower trim
  1. Pull the carpet back as needed.
  1. Remove the bolts, then remove the fuel fill door opener (A) from the body.
  1. Disconnect the fuel fill door opener cable (A) from the fuel fill door opener (B).
  1. Install the fuel fill door opener in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the opener cable is connected properly.
    • Make sure the fuel fill door opens properly.
