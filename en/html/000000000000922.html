Camshaft, Rocker Arm, Camshaft Seal, and Pulley Installation
NOTE: To prevent the rocker arm assembly from coming apart, leave the camshaft holder bolts in the holes.
  1. Reassemble the rocker arm assembly.
  1. After wiping down the camshaft and the journals in the cylinder head, lubricate both surfaces and install the camshaft.
  1. Clean and install the oil control orifice (A) with a new O-ring (B).
  1. Turn the camshaft until its keyway (C) is facing up (No. 1 piston TDC).
  1. Loosen all the valve adjusting screws.
  1. Apply liquid gasket P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S, evenly to the head mating surface (shaded areas) of the No. 1 and No. 5 camshaft holders.
    NOTE: Do not install the parts if 4 minutes or more have elapsed since applying liquid gasket. Instead, reapply liquid gasket after removing old residue.
  1. Set the rocker arm assembly in place and loosely install the bolts. Make sure that the rocker arms are properly positioned on the valve stems.
  1. Tighten each bolt two turns at a time, in the sequence shown to ensure that the rockers do not bind on the valves.

    Specified Torque

    8 mm Bolts:
    20 N·m (2.0 kgf·m, 14 lbf·ft)
    Apply engine oil to the bolt threads.
    6 mm Bolts:
    12 N·m (1.2 kgf·m, 8.7 lbf·ft)
    6 mm Bolts:
    (11), (12), (13), (14)

  1. Align the marks (A) on the new cylinder head plug (B) with the top edge of the head, then install the cylinder head plug in the cylinder head.
  1. Install the back cover, then install the camshaft pulley.
