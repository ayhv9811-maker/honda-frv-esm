Front Center Seat Turn Over Cushion Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Right recline cover
    • Left recline cover
  1. Reverse the turn over cushion, then remove the cushion tray.
  1. Remove the bolts from both sides of the turn over cushion.
  1. Stand the turn over cushion (A). Slant the cushion by sliding its pin (B) in a gloove of the hinge trim (C) forward, and sliding opposite pin (D) backward, then remove these pins out of each goolve, and remove the cushion.
  1. Install the cushion in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure each side pin is installed in a gloove of the hinge cover securely.
    • Apply liquid thread lock to the cushion mounting bolts before reinstallation.
