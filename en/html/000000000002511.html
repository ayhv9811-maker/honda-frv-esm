CKP Sensor Replacement
  1. Remove the shroud.
  1. Remove the A/C compressor.
    NOTE: Do not disconnect the A/C lines.
  1. Remove the compressor bracket.
  1. Disconnect the CKP sensor connector (A).
  1. Remove the bolt (B).
  1. Remove the CKP sensor (C).
  1. Install the sensor in the reverse order of removal with a new O-ring (D).
