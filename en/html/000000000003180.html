DTC Troubleshooting: P245D (186)
DTC P245D:
 EGR Cooler Bypass Control Solenoid Valve Circuit Shorted to Power
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Do the EGR COOLER BYPASS VALVE TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P245D indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve 2P connector.
  1. At solenoid side, measure resistance between the EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve 2P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 2.
Is there about 37−44 Ω at 20 °C (68 °F)?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 2 minutes.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve 2P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 77, then go to Step 13 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Replace the EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Do the EGR COOLER BYPASS VALVE TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P245D indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Do the EGR COOLER BYPASS VALVE TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P245D indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the EGR cooler bypass control solenoid valve and the ECM. If the ECM was updated, substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the ECM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
