Intermediate Shaft Installation
  1. Install the new set ring.
  1. Clean the areas where the intermediate shaft contacts the differential thoroughly with solvent or brake cleaner, and dry with compressed air.
    NOTE: Do not wash the rubber parts with solvent.
  1. Install the intermediate shaft (A); insert the intermediate shaft horizontally into the differential to prevent damaging the differential oil seal (B).
  1. Install the lower torque rod bracket (C), and secure it with the new bolts.
  1. Secure the lower torque rod with the new bolts.
  1. Install the right driveshaft over the intermediate shaft.
  1. Refill the transmission with the recommended transmission fluid.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle.
