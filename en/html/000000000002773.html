DTC Troubleshooting: P2228 (13)
DTC P2228:
 BARO Sensor Circuit Low Voltage
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot, and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check the BARO SENSOR in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is about 43 kPa (323 mmHg, 12.7 in.Hg), or 1.31 V or less indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Update the ECM/PCM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM/PCM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P2228 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the ECM/PCM. If the ECM/PCM was updated, substitute a known-good ECM/PCM, then recheck. If the ECM/PCM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
If the ECM/PCM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM/PCM was substituted, replace the original ECM/PCM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
