Steering Gearbox Mount Cushion Replacement
  1. Remove the steering gearbox.
  1. Position the 36 mm socket wrench (A) on the flange part of the gearbox housing with the 14, 17 mm socket wrench (B), 10 x 140 mm flange bolt (C) and the 10 mm nut (D) as shown.
  1. Hold the flange bolt with a wrench, and tighten the nut with a wrench. Remove the gearbox mount cushion (E).
  1. Apply a mild detergent solution to the new gearbox mount cushion surface (A), then place it on the gearbox mounting cushion hole.
  1. Position the 36 mm socket wrench on the flange part of the gearbox housing with the 14, 17 mm socket wrench, flange bolt, and the nut as shown.
  1. Install the gearbox mount cushion by tightening the nut until the mount cushion edges (B) properly fit on the gearbox flange surface.
