Fuel Fill Door Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Release the clip (A), then remove the cap (B) by turning it counterlockwise.
  1. Remove the screw (C).
  1. From under the rear bumper, release the drain tube (A) from the tube clip (B).
  1. Remove the fuel fill door (C) with the drain tube from the body.
  1. If necessary, disconnect the drain tube (A) from the fuel fill door (B).
  1. Install the fuel fill door in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the drain tube is connected securely.
    • Pull the lower end of the drain tube (A) down fully, then fasten the tube with the tube clip (B). And make sure that water is drained through the tube.
