Front Fender Enclosure Replacement
  1. Remove the front inner fender as needed.
  1. Open the front door. Remove the upper clip, and from inside the door, remove the lower clip securing the front fender enclosure (A) and front fender (B).
  1. From the wheel arch, release the clip and the maintenance plug (A).
  1. Remove the front fender enclosure (A).
  1. Install the fender enclosure in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Before installing the clips in the door opening area, install the front fender enclosure (A) to the front fender (B) properly as shown.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
