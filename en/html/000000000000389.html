Seat Heater Test
  1. Remove the driver's or front passenger's seat.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector (A) and 2P connector (B) from the seat heater.
  1. Check for continuity between the B1 and B2 terminals of the seat-back heater 2P connector. There should be continuity.
  1. Check for continuity between the A1 and B1 terminals, the A2 and A3 terminals, and the A2 and B2 terminals. There should be continuity.
  1. If the continuity check is not as specified, replace the appropriate seat assembly.
