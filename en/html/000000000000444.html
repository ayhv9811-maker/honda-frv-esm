Windshield Wiper Motor Test
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector (A) from the wiper motor (B).
  1. Test the motor by connecting battery power to the No. 2 terminal and ground the No. 1 [3] terminal of the wiper motor 5P connector. The motor should run at low speed. If the motor does not run or fails to run smoothly, replace the motor.
  1. Test the motor by connecting battery power to the No. 2 terminal and ground the No. 4 [5] terminal of the wiper motor 5P connector. The motor should run at high speed. If the motor does not run or fails to run smoothly, replace the motor.
