A/T Clutch Pressure Control Solenoid Valve A Replacement
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing and intake air duct.
  1. Disconnect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A connector, and remove the bolts securing the harness clamp brackets and ATF cooler inlet line brackets.
  1. Remove the mounting bolts and A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A.
  1. Remove the ATF pipe (B), ATF joint pips (C), O-rings (D), and gasket (E).
  1. Clean the mounting surface and fluid passage of the solenoid valve body and transmission housing.
  1. Install the new gasket on the transmission housing, and install the ATF pipe and ATF joint pipes. Install the new O-rings over the ATF joint pipes.
  1. Install A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A.
  1. Check the connector for rust, dirt, or oil, then connect the connector securely.
  1. Secure the ATF cooler inlet line and harness clamp brackets with the bolts.
  1. Install the intake air duct and air cleaner housing.
