A/C Signal Circuit Troubleshooting
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Turn on an air conditioner.
  1. Check the A/C CLUTCH in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is it ON?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Check the A/C system.
Does the A/C system operate?
YES -
The air conditioning system circuit is OK.■
NO -
Go to Step 5 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Activate the A/C CLUTCH in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
Is there a clicking noise from the A/C compressor clutch?
YES -
Do the A/C system test. ■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Momentarily connect A/C compressor clutch relay 4P connector terminal No. 4 to body ground with a jumper wire.
Is there clicking noise from the A/C compressor clutch?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM (A10) and the A/C compressor clutch relay.■
NO -
Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM, replace the original ECM. ■

YES -

Go to Step 13 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector E (31P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector terminal E28 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM (E28) and the A/C pressure switch.■
NO -
