Engine Mount Replacement - Upper Torque Rod
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the radio and navigation system, then write down the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then the positive cable.
  1. Remove the expansion tank bracket mounting bolt, then move the expansion tank.
  1. Remove the ground cable (A), then remove the upper torque rod (B).
  1. Install the new upper torque rod, then tighten the new bolts in the numbered sequence shown.
  1. Install the ground cable (A).
  1. Install the expansion tank.
  1. Install the battery. Clean the battery posts and cable terminals with sandpaper, then assemble them and apply grease to prevent corrosion.
  1. Enter the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
  1. Set the clock.
