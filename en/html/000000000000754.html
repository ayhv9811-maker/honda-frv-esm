M/T Assembly Removal
NOTE: Use fender covers to avoid damaging painted surfaces.
  1. Write down the frequencies for the radio's preset buttons. Disconnect the negative (âˆ’) cable first, then the positive (+) cable from the battery. Remove the battery.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the battery tray (A).
  1. Disconnect the transmission ground cable (B).
  1. Disconnect the back-up light switch connector (A), the output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor connector (B), and the reverse lockout solenoid connector (C).
  1. First remove the cable bracket (A), then disconnect the cables (B) from the top of the transmission housing. Carefully remove both cables and the bracket together so as not to bend the cables.
  1. Carefully remove the slave cylinder so as not to bend the clutch line. Do not operate the clutch pedal once the slave cylinder has been removed.
  1. Remove the harness cover.
  1. Remove the water pipe mounting bolts (A) and loosen the air cleaner housing mounting bracket bolt (B). Lower the water pipe slightly.
  1. Attach the engine hanger (C) to the engine using the 8 x 1.25 mm bolt (D).
  1. Attach the chain hoist to the engine.
  1. Remove the two upper transmission mounting bolts.
  1. Remove the transmission mount bracket (A) and transmission mounting bolt (B).
  1. Remove the air cleaner bracket (C).
  1. Raise the vehicle, and make sure it is securely supported.
  1. Drain the transmission fluid. Reinstall the drain bolt with a new washer.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the three way catalytic converter (TWC) assembly.
  1. Remove the driveshafts.
  1. Remove the intermediate shaft.
  1. Remove the front engine mount bracket mounting bolt.
  1. Remove the three bolts securing the transmission rear mount.
  1. Support the subframe with a 4 x 4 x 40 in. piece of wood and a jack.
  1. Make reference marks (A) front suspension subframe (B) and mounting bolts (C), then remove the front suspension subframe.
  1. Remove the clutch cover.
  1. Remove the front engine mount.
  1. Place the transmission jack under the transmission, and remove the transmission mounting bolts.
  1. Pull the transmission away from the engine until the transmission mainshaft clears the clutch pressure plate, then lower transmission on the transmission jack.
  1. Remove the transmission rear mount (A) and the transmission rear mount bracket (B).
  1. Remove the boot (A), the release fork (B), and the release bearing (C) from the transmission (D).
