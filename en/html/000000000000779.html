DTC Troubleshooting: P0141 (65)
DTC P0141:
 Secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) Heater Circuit Malfunction
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC P0141 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and the ECM.■
  1. Check the No. 7 IG1 FUEL PUMP (15 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Repair short in the wire between the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and the No. 7 IG1 FUEL PUMP (15 A) fuse. Also replace the fuse, then go to Step 26 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 6P connector.
  1. At the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) side, measure resistance between secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 6P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5.
Is there 5.0−7.0 Ω at room temperature?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Go to Step 23 .
  1. At the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) side, check for continuity between body ground and secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 6P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 23 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 6P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 5.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Jump the SCS line with the HDS.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector E (31P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector terminal E21 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM(E21) and the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2), then go to Step 24 .
NO -
Go to Step 30 .
  1. Measure voltage between secondary HO2S(Sensor 2) 6P connector terminal No. 5 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 17 .
NO -
Check the No. 7 IG1 FUEL PUMP (15 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box. If the fuse is OK, repair open in the wire between the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and No. 7 IG1 FUEL PUMP (15 A) fuse, then go to Step 24 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Jump the SCS line with the HDS.
  1. Reconnect the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) 6P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector E (31P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between ECM connector terminal A5 and E21.
Is there 0.1 V or less?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM (E21) and the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2), then go to Step 24 .
NO -
Go to Step 30 .
  1. Replace the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2).
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Do the ECM idle learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0141 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Go to Step 29 .
  1. Monitor the OBD STATUS for DTC P0141 in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is it PASSED?
YES -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
NO -
If it is FAILED, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If it is NOT COMPLETED, go to Step 27 and recheck.
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0141 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the secondary HO2S (Sensor 2) and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. ■
