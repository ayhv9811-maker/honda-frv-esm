Dashboard Beverage Holder Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Center lower panel
    • Dashboard center lower cover
  1. Remove the screws (A) and clip (B) securing the beverage holder (C).
  1. Remove the beverage holder (A).
  1. Install the beverage holder in the reverse order or removal, and replace the clip if it is damaged.
