Roof Molding Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the handy remover set or equivalent when prying components.
Without Roof Rail Model
Removal
  1. Remove the these items:
    • Hood hinge cover
    • Tailgate hinge cover
  1. Start the rear to the front, slowly pull the roof molding (A) up to release the molding with the clips (B) from the stud bolts (C) on the body, and to release the molding from the clips (D).
  1. Remove the windshield portion of the roof molding (A).
    -1.
    Carefully insert a handy remover (B) in under the molding next to the clip (C).
    -2.
    Pry the outside edge of the roof molding at the clip portion while pushing the molding toward outside to release the hooks (D).
    -3.
    Gradually work your way down to release each of the clip portions, and release the molding from the hooks (E) on each clip.
    -4.
    Release the hooks (F, G) on the bottom clip (H) from the lower corner of the windshield (I) and the front fender trim (J).
  1. Release the hooks (A), then remove the clips (B, C) and the retainers (D) from the body.
    NOTE: Replace any damaged clips and retainers.
Installation
  1. Install the clips (A, B, C) on the roof molding (D).
  1. Install the retainers (A) and the clip (B) on the body.
  1. Install the tailgate hinge cover.
  1. Install the roof molding (A).
    -1.
    Hold the roof molding up on the roof, and align each clip to the stud bolts and retainers.
    -2.
    Reconnect the rear end of the roof molding to the front end of the tailgate hinge cover (B).
    -3.
    From the rear to the front, fit the roof molding to the clips (C), and the clips (D) to the stud bolts (E), then push on the molding until they snap into place.
    -4.
    From the top to the bottom, push the clips (F) in the windshield portion of the roof molding until they snap into place to retainers (G), then slip the bottom clip (H) under the windshield glass.
  1. Install the hood hinge cover, and push the clip and retainer portions into place securely.
With Roof Rail Model
Removal
  1. Remove the these items:
    • Hood hinge cover
    • Roof rail
  1. Slowly pull the center roof molding (A) and rear roof molding (B) up to release the molding from the clip (C), and to release the molding with the clips (D) from the stud bolts (E) on the body.
  1. Remove the windshield portion of the front roof molding (A).
    -1.
    Release the hooks (B, C) then detach the bottom clip (D).
    -2.
    Carefully insert a handy remover (E) in under the molding next to the clip (F).
    -3.
    Pry the outside edge of the molding at the clip portion while pushing the molding toward outside to release the hooks (G).
    -4.
    Gradually work your way up to release each of the clip portions, and release the molding from the hooks (H) on each clip.
  1. Release the hooks (A), then remove the clips (B, C) and the retainers (D) from the body.
    NOTE: Replace any damaged clips and retainers.
Installation
  1. Install the clips (A, B, C) and spacers (D) on the front roof molding (E) and the center roof molding (F) and the rear roof molding (G).
  1. Install the retainers (A) and the clip (B) on the body.
  1. Install the front roof molding (A), the center roof molding (B) and the rear roof molding (C):
    -1.
    Windshield portion: Push the clips (D) in the front roof molding until they snap into the retainers (E), then slip the bottom clip (F) under the windshield glass.
    -2.
    Rear portion: Hold the center molding and rear molding up on the roof, and align each clip (G) to the stud bolts (H), then push the clip portions (G, I) into place securely.
  1. Install the roof rail and hood hinge cover.
