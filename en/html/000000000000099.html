Climate Control Power and Ground Circuit Troubleshooting
  1. Check the No. 18 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -

NO -
Replace the fuse, and recheck.■
  1. Disconnect climate control unit connector B (20P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between the No. 20 terminal of climate control unit connector B (20P) and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the No. 18 fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box and the climate control unit.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 10 terminal of climate control unit connector B (20P) and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Check for an open in the wire between the climate control unit and body ground. If the wire is OK, check for poor ground at G401.■
