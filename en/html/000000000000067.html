VSA Steering Angle Sensor Replacement
NOTE: Do not damage or drop the combination switch as the steering angle sensor is sensitive to shock and vibration.
  1. Remove the steering wheel, and steering column cover.
  1. Remove the combination light switch assembly.
  1. Remove the combination light switch (A) and the wiper/washer switch (B).
  1. Replace the combination switch body complete (C).
  1. Install the combination switch in the reverse order of removal.
    NOTE: Do not remove the steering angle sensor from the combination switch body.
