Front Door Outer Handle Replacement
KG Model
NOTE: Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the door panel.
  1. Raise the glass fully.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the panel bracket (A).
  1. Disconnect the power door lock actuator connector (A).
  1. Remove the plastic cover (B), as needed.
  1. With a clip remover, disconnect the outer handle rod (A).
  1. Driver's: While pressing a middle flange portion (A) of the outer casing cover against the latch, pull both outside flanges (B) of the retainer (C) outward.
  1. Driver's: Pull the middle flange portion (A) of the outer casing cover (B) out, then disconnect the cylinder cable (C) from the latch.
  1. Remove the bolt, then remove the spacer (A).
  1. Remove the bolt securing the outer handle protector (A), then remove the protector by releasing the hook (B) and detaching the harness clip (C).
  1. If necessary, remove the special screw, then separate the door cylinder (A) and outer handle protector (B). If the retainer (C) is damaged, release the hooks (D), and replace it.
  1. While pulling the outer handle (A), remove the handle from the holes in the door panel. Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Install the handle in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the cylinder cable and each rod is connected securely.
    • Make sure the door key cylinder/door locks operate properly.
    • Make sure the door handle works properly.
    • When reinstalling the door panel, make sure the plastic cover is installed properly and sealed around its outside perimeter to seal out water.

  1. Disconnect the power door lock actuator connector (A).
