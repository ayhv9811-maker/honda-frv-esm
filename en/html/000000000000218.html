Front Bumper Lower Trim Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the front bumper lower trim (A).
    -1.
    With headlight washer: Release the washer tube (B) from hooks (C) and slits on the front bumper lower trim.
    -2.
    Remove the screws securing the front bumper lower trim.
    -3.
    Release all of the hooks (D, E), then remove the front bumper lower trim from front bumper (F) by lifting it up.
  1. Install the front bumper lower trim in the reverse order of removal. Push the hooks into place securely.
