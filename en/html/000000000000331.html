Sunroof Motor-control Unit Replacement
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Put on gloves to protect your hands. Disconnect the connector (A), and remove the screws, then remove the motor (B). Do not operate the motor before reinstallation.
  1. Install the motor in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the connector is plugged in properly.
    • Perform the sunroof calibration.
    • Check the motor operation.
