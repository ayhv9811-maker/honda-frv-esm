DTC Troubleshooting: P0511 (14)
DTC P0511:
 IAC Valve Circuit Malfunction
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0511 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the IAC valve and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the IAC valve 3P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between IAC valve 3P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the IAC valve and PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN), then go to Step 21 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and IAC valve 3P connector terminal No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the IAC valve and G101, then go to Step 20 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector A (31P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector terminal A12 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM (A12) and the IAC valve, then go to Step 20 .
NO -
Go to Step 13 .
  1. Connect IAC valve 3P connector terminal No. 3 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector terminal A12 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM (A12) and the IAC valve, then go to Step 20 .
  1. Reconnect the IAC valve 3P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between ECM connector terminal A12 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 25 .
NO -
Go to Step 18 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the IAC valve.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Do the ECM idle learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0511 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the IAC valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0511 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the IAC valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. ■
