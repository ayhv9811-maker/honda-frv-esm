Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Rear Side Lower Area
Trunk Side Trim Panel
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to scratch the trunk side trim panel and related parts.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Rear door sill inner trim
    • Tailgate weatherstrip
    • Rear trim panel
    • Rear floor lid assembly
    • Trunk spacer
    • Jack, right side
  1. Remove the tonneau cover (A).
  1. Pry out the lid (A) and remove the bolt (B), then remove the tie down hook (C) (two places).
  1. Remove the trunk side trim panel (A).
    -1.
    Release the clip (B).
    -2.
    Release the rear lower hook (C) from the body.
    -3.
    Pull out along the front of the panel from the front door opening seal (D).
    -4.
    Pull the upper edge of the panel back to release the upper hooks (E) from the rear pillar trim (F).
    -5.
    Detach the clips (G), and pull the panel back to remove it from the rear side seat striker (H).
    -6.
    Disconnect the rear accessory socket connector (I).
  1. Remove the screws and release the hooks (A) and tabs (B), then remove the tonneau cover holder (C) and tonneau cover base (D) from the trunk side trim panel (E).
  1. Install the panel in the reverse order of removal.
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
