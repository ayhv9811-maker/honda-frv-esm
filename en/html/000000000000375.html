Rear Center Seat Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to scratch the body or tear the seat covers.
  1. Pull the rear center seat belt buckle (A) out through the buckle holder (B) and release the wire tie (C) fastening the rear center seat belt detachable anchor (D).
  1. Remove both rear center leg foot covers (A). Remove the bolts from both foot brackets (B) and the front of both seat tracks.
  1. Fold the rear center seat and both rear side seats down.
  1. Pull back the front of the center floor lid assembly to detach the clips from the seat-back, then remove it. Then remove both trunk spacers.
  1. Pull the rear center seat belt buckle and rear center seat belt detachable anchor out through each buckle holder on both sides of the seat.
  1. Remove the mounting nuts, then remove the rear center seat (A) through the rear door opening.
    NOTE: When removing the seat, grasp both seat tracks, and hold the seat in your arms to keep the seat folded. Do not catch the frame pipe (B) to remove the seat, or it may be bent.
  1. Install the seats in the reverse order of removal, and replace the wire tie with a new one.
