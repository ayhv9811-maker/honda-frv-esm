Air Cleaner Element Inspection/Replacement
  1. Remove the screws, and open the air cleaner housing cover (A).
  1. Remove the air cleaner (B) from the air cleaner housing (C).
  1. Check the air cleaner element for damage or clogging. If there is damage or clogging, clean or replace the air cleaner element.
  1. Clean and remove any debris from inside the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the air cleaner element in the reverse order of removal.
