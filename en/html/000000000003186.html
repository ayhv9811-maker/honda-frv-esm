DTC Troubleshooting: U0107 (207)
DTC U0107:
 CAN Malfunction (ISV Actuator Control Module-ECM)
NOTE:
  • Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and review the general troubleshooting information.
  • Information marked with an asterisk ( * ) applies to the E-CANL line.
  • Information marked with an asterisk ( *1 ) applies to the LHD model.
  • Information marked with an asterisk ( *2 ) applies to the RHD model.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the intake shutter valve and the ECM.■
  1. Check the No. 27 ISV (15 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Go to Step 6 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the intake shutter valve 10P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between intake shutter valve 10P connector terminal No. 8 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the intake shutter valve and the ISV control relay. Also replace the No. 27 ISV (15 A) fuse, then go to Step 36 .
NO -
Replace the No. 27 ISV (15 A) fuse, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the intake shutter valve 10P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and measure voltage between intake shutter valve 10P connector terminal No. 8 and body ground within 1 second.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 24 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the ISV control relay from the relay holder.
  1. Test the ISV control relay.
Is the ISV control relay OK?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Replace the ISV control relay, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Measure voltage between ISV control relay 4P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ISV control relay and the No. 27 ISV (15 A) fuse, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between ISV control relay 4P connector terminal No. 4 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 18 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ISV control relay and PGM-FI main relay 2, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Connect intake shutter valve 10P connector terminal No. 8 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ISV control relay 4P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 21 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the intake shutter valve and the ISV control relay, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Remove the jumper wire from intake shutter valve 10P connector.
  1. Connect ISV control relay 4P connector terminal No. 3 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 43 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 41 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ISV control relay and ECM connector No. 2 terminal No. 43, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and intake shutter valve 10P connector terminals No. 4 and No. 9 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 26 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the intake shutter valve and G101, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Connect between intake shutter valve 10P connector terminals No. 1 * , No. 6 * to body ground with a jumper wire individually.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 1 terminals No. 71 * , No. 95 and body ground individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 29 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the intake shutter valve and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 71 * (No. 95), then go to Step 36 .
  1. Substitute a known-good intake shutter valve.
  1. Reconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 42 .
NO -
Replace the original intake shutter valve, then go to Step 36 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the intake shutter valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0107 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the intake shutter valve and the ECM. If the ECM was updated, substitute a known-good ECM, then go to Step 43 . If the ECM was substituted, go to Step 1 .
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
