A/T Interlock System - Shift Lock Stop, Shift Lock Stop Cushion Replacement
NOTE: Refer to the Shift Lever Disassembly and Reassembly RHD model as needed during the following procedures.
RHD Model
  1. Remove the shift lever assembly.
  1. Remove the harness band.
  1. Pry the lock covers (A) from the D3 switch/shift lock solenoid/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector (B), and remove the covers.
  1. Remove the terminal (C) from the connector by pushing the lock tab (D) in the connector using a thin blade screwdriver (E). Remove all terminals, and replace the connector.
  1. Remove the A/T gear position indicator panel from the shift lever bracket.
  1. Remove the shift lock release (A) and release spring (B).
  1. Remove the shift lock solenoid (A) by expanding its locks, and remove the shift lock solenoid plunger (B) and plunger spring (C).
  1. If replacing the shift lock stop or shift lock stop cushion, remove the shift lock stop (D), and remove the shift lock stop cushion (E).
  1. Replace the shift lock solenoid.
  1. Apply silicone grease to the mounting shaft of the shift lock stop, and install the shift lock stop and stop cushion. Be sure to install the shift lock stop cushion on the stop.
  1. Apply silicone grease to joint of the shift lock solenoid plunger and shift lock stop.
  1. Install the shift lock solenoid plunger and plunger spring in the shift lock solenoid.
  1. Install the shift lock solenoid by aligning the joint of the shift lock solenoid plunger with the tip of the shift lock stop, then push the shift lock solenoid into the shift lever securely.
  1. Apply silicone grease to the mounting shaft of the shift lock release, and install the shift lock release and release spring.
  1. Install the D3 switch harness (WHT) terminals (A) in the No. 5 and No. 6 cavities. Either D3 switch harness terminal can be installed in the No. 5 and No. 6 cavities.
  1. Install the A/T gear position indicator panel light harness (RED/BLK) terminals (B) in the No. 1 and No. 4 cavities. Either indicator panel light harness terminal can be installed in the No. 1 and No. 4 cavities.
  1. Install the park pin switch harness (GRN) terminals (C) in the No. 2 and No. 3 cavities. Either park pin switch harness terminal can be installed in the No. 2 and No. 3 cavities.
  1. Install the shift lock solenoid RED harness terminal (D) in the No. 7 cavity, and shift lock solenoid BLK harness terminal (E) in the No. 8 cavity.
  1. Make sure that all eight terminals lock securely, then install the lock covers (F) securely on the D3 switch/shift lock solenoid/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector (G).
  1. Route the D3 switch harness (A) along the harness guide (B), place the mark (C) of the D3 switch harness on the guide, and route the shift lock solenoid harness, park pin switch harness, and indicator panel light harness along the D3 switch harness.
  1. Take a slack out of the harnesses, and tie the harnesses with the new band (D) at the guide.
  1. Install the shift lever assembly.
