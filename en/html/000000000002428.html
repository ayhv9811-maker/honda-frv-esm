Charging System Indicator Circuit Troubleshooting
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
Does the charging system indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 2 .
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Start the engine.
Does the charging system indicator go off?
YES -
Charging system indicator circuit is OK. Go to the Alternator and Regulator Circuit Troubleshooting. ■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Do the gauge assembly self-diagnostic function procedure.
Does the charging system indicator flash?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Replace the gauge assembly. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the alternator 4P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Start the engine.
Does the charging system indicator go off?
YES -
Repair a short in the wire between the alternator and gauge assembly.■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Measure the voltage between alternator 4P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to the Alternator and Regulator Circuit Troubleshooting. ■
NO -
Check for a blown No. 8 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box. If the fuse is OK, repair open in the wire between the alternator and under-dash fuse/relay box.■
