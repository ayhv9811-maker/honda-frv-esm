Rear Center Seat Cushion Cable Unit Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the rear center seat.
  1. Remove the seat cushion cover, as needed.
  1. Disconnect the cushion cable (A) at both seat tracks (B) and slide lever (C), then remove the unit.
    -1.
    Disconnect the cables from the slide lever, and detach them from the cable holder (D).
    -2.
    Detach both cables from the brackets (E), and disconnect them from both seat tracks.
    -3.
    Pull the cables out through the cable band (F) on the seat cushion cover.
    -4.
    Release both hook strips (G).
  1. Install the unit in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the cables are connected securely.
    • Check the recline operation.
