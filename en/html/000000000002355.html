M/T Shift Arm Clearance Inspection
  1. Measure the clearance between the shift arm (A) and shift piece (B) with a feeler gauge (C). If the clearance is more than the service limit, go to Step 2 .
    Standard:
    0.05−0.25 mm (0.002−0.010 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.5 mm (0.020 in.)

  1. Measure the width of the shift arm.
    • If the width is not within the standard, replace the shift arm.
    • If the width is within the standard, replace the shift piece.
    Standard:
    13.9−14.0 mm (0.54−0.55 in.)
