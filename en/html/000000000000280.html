Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Door Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to scratch the trim and panels.
Front Door Sill Area/Rear Door Sill Area
  1. Remove the front door sill inner trim (A).
    -1.
    Disengage the hooks (B) and tabs (C) from the kick panel (D) and center pillar lower trim (E).
    -2.
    Detach the clips (F), then remove the trim from the front door opening seal (G).
  1. If necessary, detach the clips (H) by pulling the front door sill outer trim (I) up, then remove it.
  1. Remove the kick panel (A). RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
    -1.
    Remove the driver's under cover or the passenger's under cover.
    -2.
    While pulling the edge of the panel away from the front door opening seal (B), and on driver's side, pulling the hood opener knob (C) back, remove the panel by detaching the clips.
  1. Remove the rear door sill inner trim (A).
    -1.
    Disengage the hooks (B) and tabs (C) from the center pillar lower trim (D) and trunk side trim panel (E).
    -2.
    Detach the clips (F, G), then remove the trim from the rear door opening seal (H).
  1. If necessary, detach the clips (I) by pulling the front door sill outer trim (J) up, then remove it.
  1. Remove the center pillar lower trim.
  1. Pull out the front door opening seal (A) from the front door opening flange all the way around.
  1. Pull the front edge of the trunk side trim panel (A) away as needed, and then pull out the rear door opening seal (B) from the rear door opening flange all the way around.
  1. Install the trim and panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clip portions into place securely.
