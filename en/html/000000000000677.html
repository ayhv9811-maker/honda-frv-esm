M/T Assembly Removal
NOTE: Use fender covers to avoid damaging painted surfaces.

  1. Drain transmission fluid with a shop towel covering the front and rear beams to catch any spilled fluid.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove exhaust pipe A.
  1. Remove the driveshafts.
  1. Remove the three bolts securing the transmission rear mount.
  1. Remove the transmission rear mount (A) and the transmission rear mount bracket (B).
  1. Support the subframe with a 4 x 4 x 40 in. piece of wood and a jack.
  1. Make reference marks (A) front suspension subframe (B) and mounting bolts (C), then remove the front suspension subframe.
  1. Remove the engine stiffeners (A), and clutch cover (B).
  1. Place the transmission jack under the transmission, and remove the three lower transmission mounting bolts.
  1. Pull the transmission away from the engine until the transmission mainshaft clears the clutch pressure plate, then lower transmission on the transmission jack.
