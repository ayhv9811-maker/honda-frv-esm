Alternator Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Disconnect the alternator connector (A) and BLK wire (B) from the alternator.
  1. Remove the harness connector (C) and harness clamps (D) from the alternator.
  1. Remove the alternator.
Installation
  1. Install the alternator.
  1. Connect the alternator connector (A) and BLK wire (B) to the alternator.
  1. Install the harness connector (C) and harness clamps (D) to the alternator.
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Connect the positive cable to the battery first, then connect the negative cable.
  1. Set the clock.
