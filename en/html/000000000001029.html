Oil Pump Removal
  1. Drain the engine oil.
  1. Remove the timing belt.
  1. Remove the oil pan.
  1. Remove the oil screen (A).
  1. Remove the mounting bolts and the oil pump assembly (B).
Oil Pump Inspection
  1. Remove the screws from the pump housing, then separate the housing and cover.
  1. Check the inner-to-outer rotor radial clearance between the inner rotor (A) and outer rotor (B). If the inner-to-outer rotor radial clearance exceeds the service limit, replace the oil pump.
    Inner Rotor-to-Outer Rotor Radial Clearance
    Standard (New):
    0.06−0.18 mm (0.002−0.007 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.20 mm (0.008 in.)

  1. Check the housing-to-rotor axial clearance between the rotor (A) and pump housing (B). If the housing-to-rotor axial clearance exceeds the service limit, replace the oil pump.
    Housing-to-Rotor Axial Clearance
    Standard (New):
    0.02−0.07 mm (0.001−0.003 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.15 mm (0.006 in.)

  1. Check the housing-to-outer rotor radial clearance between the outer rotor (A) and pump housing (B). If the housing-to-outer rotor radial clearance exceeds the service limit, replace the pump.
    Housing-to-Outer Rotor Radial Clearance
    Standard (New):
    0.10−0.18 mm (0.004−0.007 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.20 mm (0.008 in.)

  1. Inspect both rotors and the pump housing for scoring or other damage. Replace parts if necessary.
  1. Applly liquid thread lock to the pump housing screws, then install the oil pump cover.
Oil Pump Installation
  1. Remove the old oil seal from the oil pump.
  1. The seal mating surface on the oil pump should be dry.
  1. Gently tap in the new oil seal until the special tool bottoms on the pump.
  1. Apply a light coat of grease to the crankshaft and to the lip of the seal.
  1. Remove all of the old liquid gasket from the oil pump mating surfaces, bolts, and bolt holes.
  1. Clean and dry the oil pump mating surfaces.
  1. Apply liquid gasket, P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S, evenly to the engine block mating surface of the oil pump and to the inner threads of the bolt holes.
    NOTE: Do not install the parts if 4 minutes or more have elapsed since applying liquid gasket. instead, reapply liquid gasket after removing old residue.
  1. Apply oil to the new O-ring (A)
  1. Install the dowel pins (B), then align the inner rotor with the crankshaft and install the oil pump (C).
  1. Install the oil screen (D) with a new gasket (E).
  1. Clean the excess grease off the crankshaft and check the seal for distortion.
  1. Install the oil pan.
  1. Install the timing belt.
