Horn Switch Test
  1. Remove the steering column covers.
  1. Disconnect the cable reel 13P connector or 5P connector (A) from the combination switch subharness.
  1. Using a jumper wire, connect the No. 1 terminal of the dashboard wire harness 13P connector or 5P connector to body ground.
    • If the horns sound, go to Step 4 .
    • If the horns don't sound, check these items:
      • -
      No. 14 (10 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
      -
      Horn relay.
      -
      Horns.
      -
      An open in the wire.
  1. Reconnect the cable reel 13P connector or 5P connector (A) to the dashboard wire harness.
  1. Remove the driver's airbag assembly, and disconnect the horn switch 1P positive terminal from the cable reel (B).
  1. Using a jumper wire, connect the cable reel side 1P connector (C) to body ground.
    • If the horns sound, replace the driver's airbag assembly.
    • If the horns do not sound, check these items:
      • -
      Cable reel.
      -
      An open in the wire.
