Rear Door Glass Outer Weatherstrip Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Starting at the front, pull the door glass outer weatherstrip (A) up at each portion where the inner molding (B) catches the inside edge of the window slot.
  1. Twist the door glass outer weatherstrip (A) to pull the front clip (B) out from the inside of the door, then remove the weatherstrip.
  1. Push the clip portions of the door glass outer weatherstrip into place securely.
