Rear Center Seat Recline Control Cable Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the rear center seat.
  1. Remove the seat cushion.
  1. Remove the seat-back cover/pad and silencer from the seat-back frame.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Recline cover outer, both sides
    • Recline cover inner, both sides
  1. Disconnect the recline control cable (A) at both seat tracks (B) and lock knob lever (C), then remove the cable.
    -1.
    Disconnect the cable from the lock knob lever, and detach it from the lever holder (D).
    -2.
    Detach both cables from both recline brackets (E), then disconnect them from both seat tracks.
    -3.
    Release the wire tie (F).
  1. Install the cable in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace the wire tie you removed with a new one.
    • Make sure the cables are connected securely.
    • Check the recline operation.
