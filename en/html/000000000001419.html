ECT Sensor Circuit Troubleshooting
  1. Check the malfunction indicator lamp (MIL).
Does the malfunction indicator lamp come on?
YES -
Refer to the powertrain DTCs. ■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the ECT sensor 2P connector.
  1. Disconnect climate control unit connector B (20P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between the No. 6 terminal of climate control unit connector B (20P) and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the climate control unit and the ECT sensor.■
  1. Substitute a known-good climate control unit, and recheck.
Is the symptom/indication goes away?
YES -
Replace the original climate control unit .■
NO -
Replace the climate control panel.■
