Vacuum Pump Replacement
  1. Remove the chain case and cam chain.
  1. Remove exhaust pipe A, the exhaust manifold cover, the exhaust joint cover, and the catalytic converter.
  1. Disconnect the vacuum hose.
  1. Remove the bolts (A).
  1. Remove the vacuum pump (B).
  1. Install the sensor in the reverse order of removal with a new O-ring (C).
    NOTE: When remove the vacuum pump, make sure the O-ring (D) is in place.
