Rear Side Seat Belt Replacement
NOTE: Check the rear side seat belts for damage, and replace them if necessary.
Rear Side Seat Belt
  1. Fold the rear side seat forward.
  1. Pull the edge of the carpet out from under the trunk side trim panel (B), then remove the rear side seat belt lower anchor bolt (C).
  1. Remove the bracket cover (A) by releasing the hooks (B).
  1. Remove the upper anchor bolt (A).
  1. Remove the trunk side trim panel.
  1. Remove the retractor mounting self-tapping ET screw (A) and the retractor bolt (B), then remove the rear seat belt (C) and retractor (D).
  1. If necessary remove the protector (E).
  1. Install the seat belt in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions as described.
    • Before installing the anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the rear side seat belt.
Rear Side Seat Belt Buckle
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the rear side seat belt buckle (A) from the belt guide (B).
  1. Fold the rear side seat forward, and detach the clips from the seat-back, then remove the rear floor lid assembly as needed.
  1. Remove the center anchor bolt (A), and remove the seat belt buckle (B).
  1. Install the buckle in the reverse order of removal.
