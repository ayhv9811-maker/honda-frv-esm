Sunroof Calibration
NOTE: The sunroof calibration must be performed when any of the following events have occurred.
  • The sunroof is manually moved with the battery dead or disconnected.
  • The sunroof motor is replaced with a new one.
  • Any component related to the sunroof replaced.
    • -
    Wind deflector
    -
    Sunroof glass
    -
    Sunroof seal
    -
    Sunroof glass bracket
    -
    Sunroof cables, etc.
To calibrate the sunroof, perform the following steps
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Open the sunroof to full open position and release the open button.
  1. Pull and hold the sunroof OPEN switch until the sunroof glass moves slightly back to its mechanical stop and then forward again slightly.
    • New Motor
    • Other than new motor: This will take about 13 seconds.
  1. Release the OPEN switch.
  1. Within 5 seconds, pull and hold the sunroof OPEN switch again.
  1. In about 3 seconds the sunroof glass will begin to slide closed.
  1. Continue to hold the open button until the glass moves to closed position, tilts up, and returns to the closed position again.
