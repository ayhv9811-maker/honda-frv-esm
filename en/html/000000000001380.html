Brake Booster Replacement
LHD Type
NOTE: Following illustrations show K20A1 engine model, D17A2 engine model follows same steps.
  1. Remove the master cylinder.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Disconnect the vacuum hose (A) from the brake booster.
  1. Move the clutch reservoir hose (B) behind the brake line (C).
  1. From the cabin, remove the driver's dashboard under covers in the driver's compartment.
  1. Remove the brake pedal support member (A).
  1. Remove the lock pin (A) and the joint pin (B).
  1. Remove the brake booster mounting nuts (C).
  1. Remove the brake booster (A) from the engine compartment.
    • Be careful not to damage the booster surfaces and threads of the brake booster rod and the booster stud bolts.
    • Be careful not to bend or damage the brake lines.
  1. Install the brake booster in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Adjust the pushrod clearance before installing the brake booster.
    • Tighten all the mounting hardware to the specified torque values.
    • Use a new lock pin whenever installing.
    • After installing the brake booster and master cylinder, fill the reservoir with new brake fluid from an unopened container bleed the brake system, and adjust the brake pedal height and free play.
