DTC Troubleshooting: P1324 (23)
DTC P1324:
 Knock Sensor Power Source Circuit Low Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (ll).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P1324 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the knock sensor and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the knock sensor 3P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (ll).
  1. Measure voltage between ECM connector terminals A8 and A20.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 22 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector A (31P).
  1. Connect knock sensor 3P connector terminals No. 2 and No. 3 with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector terminals A8 and A20.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Remove the jumper wire from knock sensor 3P connector.
  1. Connect knock sensor 3P connector terminal No. 2 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector terminal A20 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM (A8) and the knock sensor, then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM (A20) and the knock sensor, then go to Step 17 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the knock sensor.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (ll).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Do the ECM idle learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P1324 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the knock sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P1324 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the knock sensor and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. ■
