A/T 3rd Clutch Transmission Fluid Pressure Switch Replacement
  1. Raise the vehicle up on a lift, or apply the parking brake, block the rear wheels, and raise the front of the vehicle. Make sure it is securely supported.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Disconnect the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector, and remove the switch (A).
  1. Install the new 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch and a new sealing washer (B), and tighten the switch.
  1. Make sure there is no water, oil, dust, or foreign particles inside the connector.
  1. Connect the connector securely.
  1. Install the splash shield.
