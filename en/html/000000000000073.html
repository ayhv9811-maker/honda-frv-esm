Yaw Rate/Lateral Acceleration Sensor Replacement
NOTE:
  • Do not damage or drop the sensor as it is sensitive.
  • Do not use an impact wrench.
  1. Remove the passenger's dashboard under cover.
  1. Turn up the floor carpet.
  1. Disconnect the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor 4P connector (A).
  1. Remove the sensor mounting bolts, and remove the yaw rate/lateral acceleration sensor (B).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
