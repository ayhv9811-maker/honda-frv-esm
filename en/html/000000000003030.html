A/T D3 Switch Test
RHD Model
  1. Remove the following parts:
    • Dashboard center middle panel
    • Driver's middle panel
    • Center lower panel
  1. Disconnect the D3 switch/shift lock solenoid/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector.
  1. Push the D3 switch several times, and check the switch for continuity between D3 switch/shift lock solenoid/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector terminals No. 5 and No. 6. There should be toggled continuity and no continuity between terminals every time the D3 switch is pushed.
  1. D3 switch test is finished if the test result is OK. If the D3 switch works incorrectly, replace the D3 switch.
  1. Install the following parts:
    • Center lower panel
    • Driver's middle panel
    • Dashboard center middle panel
