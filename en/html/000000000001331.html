Intermediate Shaft Installation
K20A9 engine model
  1. Use solvent or brake cleaner to thoroughly clean the areas where the intermediate shaft (A) contacts the transmission (differential), and dry with compressed air. Insert the intermediate shaft assembly into the differential. Hold the intermediate shaft horizontal to prevent damage to the differential oil seal (B).
  1. Install the flange bolt (A) and two dowel bolts (B).
  1. Install the right driveshaft.
