M/T Assembly Reassembly
NOTE: Prior to reassembling, clean all the parts in solvent, dry them, and apply lubricant to any contact surfaces.
  1. Install the magnet (A) and differential assembly (B).
  1. Install the 28 mm spring washer (A) and 28 mm washer (B) over the ball bearing (C). Note the installation direction of the spring washer (A).
  1. Apply tape on the mainshaft spline (D) to protect the seal. Install the mainshaft and countershaft (E) into the shift forks (F), and install them as an assembly.
  1. Install the reverse shift fork.
  1. Install the reverse idler gear (A) and the reverse gear shaft (B) by aligning the mark (C) with the reverse gear shaft hole (D).
  1. Select the proper size 72 mm shim (A) according to the measurements made during the mainshaft thrust clearance adjustment. Install the oil gutter plate (B), oil guide plate M, and the 72 mm shim into the transmission housing (C).
  1. Remove any dirt and oil from the tramsmission housing sealing surface. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M) to the sealing surface. Make sure you seal the entire circumference of the bolt holes to prevent oil leakage.
    NOTE: If 5 minutes have passed after applying liquid gasket, reapply it, and assemble the housings. Allow it to cure at least 20 minutes after assembly before filling the transmission with oil.
  1. Install the 14 x 20 mm dowel pins (A).
  1. Place the transmission housing over the clutch housing, being careful to line up the shafts.
  1. Lower the transmission housing the rest of the way as you expand the 72 mm snap ring (B). Release the snap ring so it seats in the groove of the countershaft bearing.
  1. Check that the 72 mm snap ring is securely seated in the groove of the countershaft bearing.
    Dimension (1) as installed:
    3.3−6.0 mm (0.13−0.24 in.)

  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M) to the threads of the 32 mm sealing cap (C), and install it on the transmission housing.
  1. Install the harness bracket (A), the transmission hanger A (B), the transmission hanger B (C), and the 8 mm flange bolts finger-tight.
  1. Tighten the 8 mm flange bolts in a crisscross pattern in several steps.
    8 x 1.25 mm
    27 N·m (2.8 kgf·m, 20 lbf·ft)

  1. Remove any dirt and oil from the change lever cover sealing surface. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M) to the sealing surface.
    NOTE: If 5 minutes have passed after applying liquid gasket, reapply it, and assemble the housings. Allow it to cure at least 20 minutes after assembly before filling the transmission with oil.
  1. Install the 8 x 14 mm dowel pins (A), clutch line clip bracket (B), and change lever assembly (C).
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M) to the threads of the interlock bolt (D), and install it on the transmission housing.
  1. Install the drain plug (A), filler plug (B), 10 mm flange bolt (C), output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor (D), plain washer (E), and O-ring (F).
  1. Install the detent bolts (A), the springs, and the steel balls with new washers.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M) to the threads of the back-up light switch (B), and install it on the transmission housing.
  1. Install the 20 mm bolt (A) and new 20 mm washer (B).
