Tailgate Spoiler Trim Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the tailgate spoiler trim and tailgate.
  1. From inside the tailgate, remove the bolts.
  1. Remove the grommet (A), and remove the clip by pinching its tabs.
  1. Remove the tailgate spoiler trim (A).
    -1.
    Close the tailgate, then lift the tailgate spoiler trim (A) to detach the clips.
    -2.
    Disconnect the rear window wiper washer tube (B).
    -3.
    Remove the grommet (C) from the tailgate, then disconnect the high mount brake light connector (D).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips with new ones.
    • Make sure the rear window wiper washer tube is connected securely, and the high mount brake light connector is plugged in properly.
