Dashboard Center Lower Panel Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • RHD is shown, LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Dashboard center middle panel
    • Driver's middle panel, RHD
    • Dashboard center lower cover
  1. Remove the bolts securing the parking brake knob (A), then lower the knob down. Take care not to damage the parking brake cable.
  1. Remove the center lower panel (A).
    -1.
    Open the beverage holder (B).
    -2.
    Release the clip (C).
    -3.
    Detach the clips (D, E) by pulling the panel back.
    -4.
    Disconnect the cigarette lighter connector (F), and ashtray light bulb socket (G).
  1. Install the panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips with new ones.
    • Make sure the connector is plugged in properly, and the bulb socket is connected securely.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
