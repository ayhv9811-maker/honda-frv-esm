  1. Remove the differencial assembly.
  1. Remove the ball bearing (A) from the clutch housing (B) using the special tool.
  1. Remove the oil seal (A) from the clutch side. Be careful when removing the seal so the clutch housing is not damaged.
  1. Drive the new oil seal in from the transmission side using the special tools.
  1. Drive the new ball bearing (A) in from the transmission side using the special tools.
