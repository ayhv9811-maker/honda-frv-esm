Rear Center Seat Slide Lever Unit Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the slide lever trim.
  1. Remove the seat cushion cover, as needed.
  1. Disconnect the slide cables (A) from the slide lever unit (B).
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the slide lever unit (A) from the seat cushion frame.
  1. Remove the screw. Release the E clip (A), pull out the shaft (B), then remove the slide lever (C) from the slide lever unit (D).
  1. Install the unit in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace the E clip you removed with a new one.
    • Apply liquid thread to the slide lever unit mounting screw before reinstallation.
    • Make sure the slide cables are connected securely.
