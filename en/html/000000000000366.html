Navigation Unit DVD-ROM Replacement
NOTE: When the DVD-ROM is re-inserted or replaced, a map match must be done.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Press the EJECT button (A) of the navigation unit (B) located under the passenger's seat.
  1. Remove the DVD-ROM.
  1. Insert the new DVD-ROM.
    NOTE: Do not turn the ignition switch OFF, until data is downloaded to navigation unit.
