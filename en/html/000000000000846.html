M/T Countershaft Bearing Replacement
Special Tools Required

Oil seal driver
 07JAD-PL90100

Adjustable bearing puller, 25−40 mm
 07JAC-PH80100

Bearing remover shaft
 07JAC-PH80200

Slide hammer
 07741-0010201
  1. Remove the bearing set plate (A) from the clutch housing (B).
  1. Remove the needle bearing (A) using the special tools, then remove the oil guide plate C (B).
  1. Position the oil guide plate C (A) and new needle bearing (B) in the bore of the clutch housing (C).
  1. Install the needle bearing using the special tool.
  1. Install the bearing set plate (A) with the bolts (B).
