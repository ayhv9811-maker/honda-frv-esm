Washer Reservoir Replacement
  1. Remove the right inner fender.
  1. Disconnect the washer tubes (A).
  1. Disconnect the 2P connectors (B) from the washer motors (C).
  1. Remove the three mounting bolts and washer reservoir (A).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
    • Make sure the connectors and tubes connect properly.
    • Make sure the washer motors operate properly.
