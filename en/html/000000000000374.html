Rear Side Seat Removal/Installation
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body or tear the seat covers.
  1. Pull the rear side seat belt buckle out through the belt guide on the seat cushion.
  1. Remove the foot cover (A) by releasing the hooks (B), then remove the mounting bolts.
  1. Fold the rear side seat down, and pull back the front of the rear floor lid assembly (A) to detach the clips (B) from the seat-back (C).
  1. Remove the mounting bolts (D).
  1. While lifting the rear side seat (A), move it forward to remove the seat belt buckle (B) from between the seat-back and seat cushion. Remove the seat through the rear door opening.
  1. Install the seat in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
