Knuckle-Upper Arm Joint Bushing
  1. Position the knuckle (A) on the press with the machined surface (B) facing down.
  1. Adjust the special tool so it matches the inner diameter of the bushing hole, then tighten the socket bolts (C) securely.
  1. Position the special tool on the bushing (D).
  1. Remove the bushing by pressing on the special tool with a press as shown.
    NOTE: Be careful not to damage the inside of the bushing hole while pressing on the bushing.
  1. Position the knuckle (A) on the press with the machined surface (B) facing up.
  1. Align the bushing (C) to the knuckle.
    NOTE: The installed bushing's gap (D) points upward and downward to the installed knuckle.
  1. Adjust the special tool so it matches with the outer diameter of the bushing.
  1. Position the special tool on the outer sleeve (E) of the bushing.
  1. Press the bushing into the knuckle using the special tool as shown.
Knuckle-Trailing Arm Joint Bushings
  1. Remove the bushing stoppers (A).
  1. Remove the bushing inner pipe (A) from the bushing (B) using the special tool and a hydraulic press.
  1. Press the bushing (A) off from opposite side removed the bushing inner pipe completely on Step 2 using the appropriate drive extension (B) with the socket wrench (C), the special tools and a hydraulic press.
  1. Remove the outer pipe (A) using the appropriate drive extension with the socket wrench, the special tools and a hydraulic press.
  1. Install the bushings (A) and bushing stopper (B) pressed both side of each bushing hole of knuckle.
    NOTE:
    • The front side and rear side bushings are different.
    • Make sure the installation direction of bushings and bushing stoppers.
    • The installed bushing's gaps (C) points upward and downward to the installed knuckle.
