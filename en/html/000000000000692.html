M/T Shift Arm Cover Assembly Disassembly/Reassembly
Special Tools Required

Attachment, 32 x 35 mm
 07746-0010100

Driver
 07749-0010000
  1. Remove the breather cap (A), detent bolt (B), washer (C), spring (D), and steel ball (E).
  1. Remove the special bolt (A) and spring washer (B).
  1. Remove the shift lever (C), shift arm C (D), and thrust washer (E) from the shift arm cover (F).
  1. Remove the oil seal (G).
    NOTE: When installing, turn alloy surface of the thrust washer to the shift arm cover side.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
    NOTE: Install the oil seal (A) using the special tools.
