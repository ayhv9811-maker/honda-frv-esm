Transmission Mount Bracket Removal/Installation - Upper
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the radio and navigation system, then write down the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then the positive cable.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Remove the engine under cover.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the hoist.
  1. Support the engine with a jack and wood block under the oil pan.
  1. Remove the battery.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the battery base.
  1. Remove the transmission upper mount bracket (A).
  1. Install the transmission upper mount bracket, and tighten the new bolts, then loosely tighten the mounting bolt (B).
  1. Remove a jack.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Install the engine under cover.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the hoist.
  1. Loosen the transmission upper mount mounting bolt/nuts (A), then retighten the mounting bolt/nuts.
  1. Tighten the transmission upper mount bracket mounting bolt (B).
  1. Install the battery base.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the battery. Clean the battery posts and cable terminals with sandpaper, then assemble them and apply grease to prevent corrosion.
  1. Enter the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
  1. Set the clock.
