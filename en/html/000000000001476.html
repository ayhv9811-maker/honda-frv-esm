Rear Center Seat-back Cover/Pad Replacement - Without Leather
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  1. Remove the rear center seat.
  1. Pull up the lock knob trim (A) to release the clip, release the trim from the pivot (B) of the lock knob and a hole (C) in the lever holder.
  1. Remove the screw while pulling the lock knob (A) up, and detach the pin (B), then remove the knob.
  1. From behind the seat-back, detach the clips, and unzip the seat-back cover (A).
  1. Pull back the seat-back cover (A), then release the hook strips (B) and clips (C, D) from the seat-back frame.
  1. Release the bottom hook (A), then pull out the seat-back pad (B) from the frame (C), then pull out the headrest guides (D) while pinching the end of the guides, and remove them. Release the seat-back cover hooks (E) at the lock knob portion.
  1. Remove the seat-back cover/pad (A) and the silencer (B) from the seat-back frame (C).
  1. Install the seat-back cover/pad in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat-back cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hook and clips.
    • Replace any cilps you removed with new ones using commercially available uphoistery ring pliers.
