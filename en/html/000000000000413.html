Door Lock Knob Switch Test
Driver's Door
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector (A) from the super locking actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 8 [7] and No. 9 [6] terminals when the door lock knob switch is LOCK position.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 10 [5] and No. 9 [6] terminals when the door lock knob switch is UNLOCK position.
    [ ]:
    KG

Passenger's/Rear Door
  1. Remove the passenger's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 10P connector (A) from the super locking actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    There should be continuity between the No. 5 [10] and No. 6 [9] terminals when the door lock knob switch is UNLOCK position.
