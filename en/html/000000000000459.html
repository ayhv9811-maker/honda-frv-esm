Power Window Master Switch Test
  1. Remove the power window master switch.
  1. Disconnect the 14P and 10P connectors from the switch.
Driver's Switch
The driver's switch is combined with the control unit so you cannot isolate the switch to test it.
Instead, run the master switch input test procedures on . If the tests are normal, the driver's switch must be faulty.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the switch.
