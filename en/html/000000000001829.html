Transmission Mount Replacement - Lower
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Remove the left front wheel.
  1. Remove the engine under cover.
  1. Remove the transmission lower mount.
  1. Install the new transmission lower mount with new bolts.
  1. Install the engine under cover.
  1. Install the left front wheel.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the hoist.
