Dashboard Passenger's Under Cover Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the passenger's under cover (A).
    -1.
    Gently pull the cover back to detach the clips (B, C).
    -2.
    Pull the cover away to release the pins (D) from holes in the kick panel (E) and dashboard center lower cover (F).
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips with new ones.
    • Make sure the pins are installed into the holse in the kick panel and dashboard center lower cover properly.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
