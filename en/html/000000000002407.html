Front Center Seat Turn Over Cushion Cover Replacement - With Leather
Leather Cushion Cover
NOTE:
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the turn over cushion.
  1. Detach the clips (A, B) by pulling the table board (C) out.
  1. Release the hook strips (A) and the clips (B), then pull back the cushion cover, and remove the strap (C) from the cushion wire (D).
  1. Remove the cushion cover/pad (A) from the cushion frame (B).
  1. Pull back the edge of the cushion cover all the way around, and release the clips (A), then separate the cushion cover (B) and pad.
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a turn over cushion cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hook and clips.
    • Using commercially available upholstery ring pliers (A), replace all of the clips you removed with new ones (B).
