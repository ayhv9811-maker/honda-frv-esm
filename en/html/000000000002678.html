MTF Inspection and Replacement
  1. Park the vehicle on level ground, and turn the engine off.
  1. Remove the filler plug (A) and sealing washer (B), check the condition of the fluid, and make sure the fluid is at the proper level (C).
  1. If the transmission fluid is dirty, remove the drain plug (A) and drain the transmission fluid (MTF).
  1. Reinstall the drain plug and a new sealing washer (B).
  1. Refill the transmission with the recommended fluid to the proper level. Always use genuine Honda Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF). Using motor oil can cause stiffer shifting because it does not contain the proper additives.
    Manual Transmission Fluid Capacity:
    2.1 L (2.2 US qt, 1.8 lmp qt) at fluid change
    2.3 L (2.4 US qt, 2.0 lmp qt) at overhaul

  1. Reinstall the filler plug and a new sealing washer.
