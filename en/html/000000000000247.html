Tailgate Support Strut Replacement
NOTE:
  • Have an assistant help you when removing and installing the support strut.
  • Take care not to scratch the body and tailgate.
  1. Remove the bolts.
  1. Remove the pivot bolt (A), then remove the support strut (B).
  1. Install the tailgate side pivot bolt first, next install the body side, and fix the support strut correctly.
