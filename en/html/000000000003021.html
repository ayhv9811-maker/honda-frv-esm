A/T Shift Lever Ring Replacement
NOTE: Refer to the Shift Lever Disassembly and Reassembly LHD model as needed during the following procedures.
LHD Model
  1. Remove the shift lever assembly.
  1. Wrap the end of a flat-tip screwdriver with tape, pry the D3 switch cover, and remove the D3 switch cover.
  1. Remove the D3 switch (A) by expanding its locks (B). Remove the screws (C), shift lever button (D), spring (E), shift lever knob (F), and shift lever knob cover (G).
  1. Remove the harness bands and tape.
  1. Pry the lock covers (A) of the D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector (B), and remove the covers.
  1. Remove the terminal (C) from the connector by pushing the lock tab (D) in the connector using a thin blade screwdriver (E). Remove all six terminals, and replace the connector.
  1. Pull the D3 switch harness (A) out of the shift lever, and remove the shift lever ring.
  1. Insert the D3 switch harness through the passage (B) of the new shift lever ring (C).
  1. Insert the D3 switch harness into the shift lever (D) and through the shift lever, and install the shift lever ring.
  1. Install the D3 switch harness terminals (WHT) (A) in the No. 1 and No. 3 cavities. Either D3 switch harness terminal can be installed in the No. 1 and No. 3 cavities.
  1. Install the park pin switch harness terminals (GRN) (B) in the No. 2 and No. 6 cavities. Either park pin switch harness terminal can be installed in the No. 2 and No. 6 cavities.
  1. Install the A/T gear position indicator panel light harness (RED/BLK) (C) in the No. 4 and No. 5 cavities. Either A/T gear position indicator panel light harness terminal can be installed in the No. 4 and No. 5 cavities.
  1. Make sure that all six terminals lock securely, then install the lock covers (D) securely on the D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector (E).
  1. Route the D3 switch harness (A) along the harness guide (B), place the mark (C) of the harness on the guide, and tie the harness and guide with the new band (D).
  1. Route the harnesses along the harness guide (E), and tie the harnesses and guide with the new band (F).
  1. Tie the harnesses together into bundles with new tape (A).
  1. Secure the harnesses with the new bands (B) at the clamp guides (C).
  1. Point the bevel (A) of the shift lever rod (B) toward the threaded holes (C), and push it into the bottom. Apply silicone grease to the top of the rod.
  1. Install the shift lever knob cover (D).
  1. Apply silicone grease to the shift lever button spring (E), and install the spring, shift lever button (F), and shift lever knob (G).
  1. Apply non-hardening thread lock sealant to screw threads, and secure the shift lever knob to the shift lever.
  1. Install the D3 switch (H) and D3 switch cover (I).
  1. Install the shift lever assembly.
