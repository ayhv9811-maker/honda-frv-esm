Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Tailgate
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  1. Remove the tailgate upper trim (A).
    -1.
    Disengage both hooks (B) and tabs (C) from the right tailgate side trim (D) and left tailgate side trim (E).
    -2.
    Detach the clips by pulling the trim back.
  1. Remove the right tailgate side trim (A) and left tailgate side trim (B).
    -1.
    Pull the upper part of the trim back to detach the clips.
    -2.
    Disengage the hooks (C) and tabs (D) from the tailgate lower trim panel (E).
  1. Remove the pull pocket (A) by pulling it out and releasing the hooks (B).
  1. Remove the tailgate lower trim panel (A).
    -1.
    Release both upper edges (B) from the tailgate.
    -2.
    Detach the clips (C) beside the tailgate latch.
    -3.
    Pull down the panel to detach all of the clips (D).
  1. Install the trim and panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
