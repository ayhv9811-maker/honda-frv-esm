DTC Troubleshooting: A2-11
DTC A2-11:
 Faulty power supply (VB line)
  1. Check the No. 2 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Replace the No. 2 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and wait for 30 seconds. Then turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check the No. 2 (10 A) fuse.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.
NO -
Go to Step 5 .
  1. Replace the No. 2 (10 A) fuse.
  1. Turn the ignition swith OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P) from the SRS unit..
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Turn the ignition swith ON (II) and wait for 30 seconds. Then turn the igunition switch OFF.
  1. Check the No. 2 (10 A) fuse.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Short to ground in the SRS unit; replace the SRS unit.■
NO -
Short to ground in the dashboard wire harness or in the under-dash fuse/relay box No. 2 (10 A) fuse circuit; replace the dashboard wire harness. If the problem is still there, replace the under-dash fuse/relay box.■

  1. Disconnect SRS unit connector A (28P) from the SRS unit..
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Connect a voltmeter between the No. 18 terminal of SRS unit connector A (28P) and body ground. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and measure the voltage. There should be battery voltage.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Faulty SRS unit or poor contact at SRS unit connector A (28P) and the SRS unit; check the connection. If the connection is OK, replace the SRS unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 15 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect under-dash fuse/relay box connector R (2P).
  1. Check resistance between the No. 1 terminal of under-dash fuse/relay box connector R (2P) and the No. 18 terminal of SRS unit connector A (28P). There should be 0−1.0 Ω.
Is the resistance as specified?
YES -
Open in the under-dash fuse/relay box or poor contact between connector R (2P) and the under-dash fuse/relay box; check the connection. If the connection is OK, replace the under-dash fuse/relay box.■
NO -
Open in the dashboard wire harness; replace the dashboard wire harness.■
