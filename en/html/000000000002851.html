  1. Install the torque converter (A) on the mainshaft (B) with the new O-ring (C).
  1. Install the 14 x 20 mm dowel pins (D) in the torque converter housing.
  1. Place the transmission on the jack, and raise the transmission to the engine level.
  1. Install the lower transmission housing mounting bolt (A) part-way in the bolt hole on the engine, then attach the transmission to the engine.
  1. Install the lower transmission housing mounting bolt (B), and tighten the bolts.
  1. Install the rear transmission housing mounting bolt.
  1. Install the front transmission housing mounting bolt.
  1. Install the upper transmission housing mounting bolts.
  1. Secure the transmission mount bracket on the transmission housing with the new mounting bolts.
  1. Install the new set rings (A) on both driveshafts (B).
  1. Clean the areas where the driveshafts contact the transmission (differential) with solvent, and dry with compressed air. Then install the driveshafts, be sure not to allow dust or other foreign particles to enter the transmission. Turn the steering knuckle fully outward, and slide the driveshaft into the differential until you feel its set ring fully engage the side gear.
  1. Attach the torque converter to the drive plate with eight bolts (A). Rotate the crankshaft pulley as necessary to tighten the bolts to 1/2 of the specified torque, then to the final torque, in a crisscross pattern. After tightening the last bolt, check that the crankshaft rotate freely.
  1. Install the torque converter cover (B).
  1. Install the shift cable cover bracket on the transmission.
  1. Install the control lever (A) over the control shaft (B).
  1. Secure the shift cable brackets (C) (D) with the bolts.
  1. Secure the control lever with the new lock washer (E) and the lock bolt (F), then bend the lock tab of the lock washer against the bolt head.
  1. Install the shift cable cover (G).
  1. Support the front subframe with a wood piece and a jack, and lift it up to the body.
  1. Loosely install the new subframe mounting bolts.
  1. Align the reference marks (A) with the center of the subframe mounting bolts (B), then tighten the mounting bolts to the specified torque.
  1. Secure the lower torque rod with the new bolts.
  1. Connect the ball joints (A) to both lower arms (B), and install the new ball joint nuts (C). Tighten the nuts, then secure the nuts with the spring clips (D). Install the spring clips from the inside of the vehicle, with their hooked sides facing the front (E) of the vehicle in the direction shown.
  1. Connect the stabilizer links (F) to the lower arms, and install the nuts (G). Insert a 5 mm Allen wrench (H) in the top of the ball joint pins (I), and tighten the nuts.
  1. Secure the ATF cooler line with the bolts on the front subframe.
  1. Connect the headlight adjuster leveling sensor connector (if equipped).
  1. Remove the hanger plate (A) and bolt (B), and install the harness cover bracket (C) on the engine.
  1. Remove the engine support hanger.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing bracket (A).
  1. Install the harness cover (B) on its bracket, and secure it with the bolts.
  1. Secure the connector bracket (C).
  1. Connect disconnected connectors (D) and vacuum tube (E), and secure the tube with the clip.
  1. Install the ACG terminal (A), connect the connectors (B), and install the harness clamp (C) on its bracket (D).
  1. Secure the harness cover with the bolts.
  1. Install the engine cover.
  1. Connect the ATF cooler hoses (A) to the ATF cooler lines (B), and secure the hoses with the clips (C).
  1. Connect the shift solenoid harness connector (A), A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve B connector (B), and A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve C connector (C), and install the harness clamps (D) on the clamp brackets (E).
  1. Connect the 3rd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch connector.
  1. Connect the output shaft (countershaft) speed sensor connector (A) and input shaft (mainshaft) speed sensor connector (B).
  1. Install the transmission range switch connector (C) on the connector bracket, and connect the connector.
  1. Connect the connector to the 2nd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (A), and secure the harness clamp brackets with the bolts.
  1. Connect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A connector (A).
  1. Secure the harness clamp bracket (B) with the bolts.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing bracket.
  1. Refill the transmission with ATF.
  1. Install the battery base and resonator.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing and intake air duct.
  1. Install the front bulkhead cover.
  1. Install the cowl cover.
  1. Install the windshield wiper arms.
  1. Install the battery tray, battery, and battery cover, then secure the battery with its hold-down bracket.
  1. Connect the battery terminals, and apply grease around the battery terminals.
  1. Set the parking brake. Start the engine, and shift the transmission through all positions three times.
  1. Check the shift lever operation, A/T gear position indicator operation, and shift cable adjustment.
  1. Check and adjust the front wheel alignment.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Check the ATF level.
  1. Perform the road test.
  1. After the road test, raise the vehicle on a hoist, and make sure it is securely supported.

  1. Loosen the transmission mount base bracket bolt (A) and nuts (B).
  1. Loosen the lower torque rod bolts (A) (B).
  1. Retighten the lower torque rod bolts (B) to the specified torque, then retighten the bolt (A) to the specified torque.
  1. Tighten the transmission mount base bracket bolt and nuts to the specified torque.
