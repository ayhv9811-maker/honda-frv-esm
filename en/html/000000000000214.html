Emblem/Sticker Replacement
NOTE: When using dental fross to cut the emblem adhesive, take care not to scratch the body.
  1. To remove the front ‘‘H'' emblem, remove the front grille and remove the front center emblem base.
  1. Clean the bonding surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol. After cleaning, keep oil, grease, and water from getting on the surface.
  1. Apply the emblem/sticker where shown:
    • When installing the rear VTEC/i-VTEC sticker on the rear window, place the sticker on the rear window as shown, then press the sticker into place, and remove the application tape.
    • FR-V emblem: Remove the adhesive backing. Put the FR-V emblem with the application guide foam on the tailgate as shown, then press the emblem into place. Remove the film and application guide foam.
