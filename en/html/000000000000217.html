Front Bumper Lid Replacement
Without Front Fog Lights
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Pull back the front inner fender, as needed.
  1. Remove the front bumper lid (A) by releasing the hooks (B).
  1. Install the front bumper lid in the reverse order of removal, push the hooks into place securely.
