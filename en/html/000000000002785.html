DTC Troubleshooting: U0155 (126) - PGM-FI System
DTC U0155:
 F-CAN Malfunction (ECM/PCM Gauge Assembly)
NOTE: Before you troubleshoot, record all freeze data and any on-board snapshot, and review the general troubleshooting information.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0155 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly and the ECM/PCM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Jump the SCS line with the HDS.
  1. Remove the gauge assembly.
  1. Disconnect the gauge control module 28P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector A (44P).
  1. Connect gauge assembly 28P connector terminal No. 11 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminal A36 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A36) and the gauge assembly, then go to Step 13 .
  1. Connect gauge assembly 28P connector terminal No. 27 to body ground with a jumper wire.
  1. Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminal A37 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Substitute a known-good gauge assembly, then go to Step 13 and recheck. If DTC U0155 is not indicated, replace the original gauge assembly, then go to Step 13 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A37) and the gauge assembly, then go to Step 13 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM with the HDS.
  1. Do the ECM/PCM idle learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC U0155 indicated?
YES -
Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the gauge assembly and the ECM/PCM, then go to Step 1 .
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete. If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.■
