A/T Idler Gear Shaft Removal and Installation
  1. Remove the snap ring (A), cotter retainer (B), and cotter keys (C). Do not distort the snap ring.
  1. Remove the idler gear shaft/idler gear assembly (D) from the transmission housing.
  1. Check the snap rings and cotter retainer for wear and damage. Replace them if they are worn, distorted, or damaged.
  1. Install the idler gear and shaft in the reverse order of removal.
