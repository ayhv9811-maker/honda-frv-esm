Power Mirror Actuator Replacement
  1. Remove the mirror holder, power mirror, and disconnect the connector.
  1. Cut the wire harness (A) with cutter.
  1. Remove the gasket (B).
  1. Remove the three screws, and separate the mirror housing (A) from the bracket (B).
  1. Remove the four screws, and the visor (A).
  1. Remove the screw and separate the actuator (A) from the housing (B), and disconnect the 2P connector (C).
  1. Remove the three screws and the actuator (A).
  1. Route the wire harness of the new actuator through the hole in the bracket and gasket.
  1. Install the actuator, bracket, harness clip, and gasket in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Install the new actuator terminals into the connector in the original arrangement.
