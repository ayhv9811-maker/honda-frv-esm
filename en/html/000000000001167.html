Engine Oil Pan Removal
  1. If the engine is out of the vehicle, go to Step 14 .
  1. Drain the engine oil.
  1. Disconnect the intake air temperature (IAT) sensor connector. Remove the breather hose and harness clamp, then remove the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Attach the chain hoist to the engine/transmission.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the hoist to full height.
  1. Remove the front tyres/wheels.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Disconnect the air fuel ratio (A/F) sensor connector and secondary heated oxygen sensor (secondary HO2S) connector, then remove the harness from the clamps.
  1. Remove the three way catalytic converter (TWC).
  1. Separate the knuckles from the lower arms.
  1. Remove the rear mount mounting bolts.
  1. Remove the front mount mounting bolt.
  1. Use a marker to make alignment marks on the reference lines that align with the centers of the rear subframe mounting bolts. Remove the front subframe.
  1. Remove the clutch cover.
  1. Drive an oil pan seal cutter between the oil pan and engine block.
  1. Cut the oil pan seal by striking the side of the cutter to slide the cutter along the oil pan.
  1. Remove the oil pan.
