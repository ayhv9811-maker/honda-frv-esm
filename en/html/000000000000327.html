Sunroof Drip Rail Replacement
  1. Remove the sunroof glass.
  1. Remove the screws securing the drip rail (A).
  1. Release the drip rail (A) from both hooks (B) of the drip link by pulling the drip rail forward.
  1. Remove the drip rail (A).
  1. Install the rail in the reverse order of removal, and check the glass height adjustment.
  1. Check for water leaks. Let the water run freely from a hose without a nozzle. Do not use a high-pressure spray.
