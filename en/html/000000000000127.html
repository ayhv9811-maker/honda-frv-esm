Rear Center Seat Belt Replacement
NOTE: Check the rear center seat belt for damage, and replace it if necessary.
Rear Center Seat Belt
  1. Remove these items:
    • Retractor cover
    • Rear pillar trim, both sides
    • Rear grab handles, both sides
    • Rear ceiling light
  1. Pull down the rear portion of the headliner. Take care not to bend the headliner excessively.
  1. Remove the upper anchor bolt (A) and the retractor mounting bolts (B), and detach the anntena lead clips (C), then remove the rear center seat belt (D) and retractor (E).
  1. Install the seat belt in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions as described.
    • Assemble the washers, collars, and bushing on the upper anchor bolt as shown.
    • Before installing the anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the rear center seat belt.
    • Apply liquid thread lock to the upper anchor bolt.
Upper anchor bolt installation
Rear Center Seat Belt Buckle/Rear Center Seat Belt Detachable Anchor
NOTE: The seat belt buckle is shown, and the seat belt detachable anchor is similar.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the rear side seat, then lift up on the seat cushion, and hold it.
  1. To remove the seat belt buckle, pull it out through the buckle holder. To remove the seat belt detachable anchor, release the wire tie fastenig the detachable anchor.
  1. Remove the center anchor bolt (A), then remove the seat belt buckle (B).
  1. Install the buckle/detachable anchor in the reverse order of removal. Assemble the washers, spring, bushing, bracket, and collar on the center anchor bolt/lower anchor bolt as shown.
Center anchor bolt/Lower anchor bolt installation
