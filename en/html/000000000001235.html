Intake Manifold Removal and Installation

Exploded View
Removal
  1. Remove the intake manifold cover.
  1. Disconnect the intake air temperature (IAT) sensor connector (A).
  1. Remove the breather hose (B) and harness clamp (C), then remove the air cleaner housing assembly (D).
  1. Remove the evaporative emission (EVAP) canister hose (A), brake booster vacuum hose (B), and positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) hose (C).
  1. Remove the water bypass hoses, then plug the water bypass hoses.
  1. Disconnect the intake manifold runner control (IMRC) solenoid valve connector (A), then remove the bolt (B) securing the IMRC solenoid valve.
  1. Remove the harness clamps.
  1. Remove the engine wire harness connectors and wire harness clamps from the intake manifold.
    • Throttle actuator connector
    • Manifold absolute pressure (MAP) sensor connector
    • EVAP canister purge valve connector
  1. Remove the intake manifold.
Installation
  1. Install the intake manifold (A) with a new gasket (B), and tighten the bolts/nuts in a crisscross pattern in two or three steps, beginning with the inner bolt.
  1. Install the harness clamps.
  1. Connect the intake manifold runner control (IMRC) solenoid valve connector (A), then install the bolt (B) securing the IMRC solenoid valve.
  1. Install the water bypass hoses.
  1. Install the EVAP canister hose (A), brake booster vacuum hose (B), and positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) hose (C).
  1. Install the air cleaner housing assembly (A), then install the the breather hose (B) and harness clamp (C).
  1. Connect the intake air temperature (IAT) sensor connector (D).
  1. Install the intake manifold cover.
  1. Refill the radiator with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
