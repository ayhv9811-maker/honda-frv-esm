Keyless Entry Transmitter Programming
Storing transmitter codes: The cords of up to three transmitters can be stored into the keyless receiver unit memory. (If a fourth code is stored, the code which was input will be erased.)
NOTE:
  • It is important to maintain the time limits between the steps. Make sure the doors, the hood, and the tailgate are crossed.
  • Perform all of the operations within 1 to 4 sec.
How to program one transmitter
Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver for three times. Then confirm you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators whenever the buttons pushed for a time.
How to program two transmitters
Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the first transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver for a time. Then confirm you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators.
Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the second transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver for two times. Then confirm you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators whenever the buttons pushed for a time.
How to program three transmitters
How to program three transmitters:
Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the first transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver for a time. Then confirm you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators.
Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the second transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver for a time. Then confirm you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators.
Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the third transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver for a time. Then confirm you can hear the sound of the door lock actuators whenever the buttons pushed for a time.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Repeat the operation of the step 1 to 3 for two times.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Press the transmitter lock or unlock button with the transmitter aimed at the keyless receiver. The door lock actuators should be operated, and the transmitter shifts to programming mode.
