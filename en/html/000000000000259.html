Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Trunk Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
Rear Trim Panel
  1. Reverse the rear floor lid assembly.
  1. Remove the tailgate weatherstrip, as needed.
  1. Remove the rear trim panel (A).
    -1.
    Disengage the tabs (B) and hooks (C) on both sides from both trunk side trim panel (D).
    -2.
    Detach the clips by pulling the panel up.
  1. Install the panel in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clips and hooks into place securely.
Rear Floor Lid
  1. Fold the rear center seat and both rear side seats forward.
  1. Remove the center floor lid assembly (A).
    -1.
    Detach the clips (B).
    -2.
    Detach the clips (C) from the frame pipe (D) of the rear center seat (E).
    -3.
    Pull the center floor lid assembly out from between the bracket (F) and base of the rear floor lid assembly (G).
  1. Detach the clips (H) from both rear side seats (I).
  1. Reverse the rear floor lid assembly, detach the clips (J) from both trunk side trim panels (K), release the fasteners (L) from the right trunk spacer (M) and from the left trunk spacer (N), then remove the rear floor lid assembly.
  1. Remove both trunk spacers.
  1. If necessary, remove the screw (A), then remove the strap (B). Remove the screws (C), then remove the rails (D) as shown. If it is damaged, replace the clip (E) fastening the bracket (F) and base (G).
  1. Install the lid in the reverse order of removal, and replace any damaged clips.
