Tailgate Latch Replacement
NOTE: If the tailgate latch doesn't be unlocked by using the keyless entry transmitter or unlocking the driver's door lock, remove the maintenance lid (A) from the tailgate lower trim panel, then unlock the tailgate latch by turning the pivot (B) of the lever clockwise as shown with a flat-tip screwdriver.
NOTE: Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the tailgate lower trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the handle cable from the tailgate handle.
  1. Disconnect the tailgate latch actuator connector (A) and tailgate latch switch connector (B), and detach the tailgate latch switch connector and cable clip (C) from the tailgate.
  1. Remove the bolts, then remove the tailgate latch (A).
  1. Install the tailgate latch in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the connectors are plugged in properly and the handle cable is connected properly.
    • Make sure the cable actuate the latch before you close the tailgate.
    • Make sure the tailgate opens properly and locks securely.
