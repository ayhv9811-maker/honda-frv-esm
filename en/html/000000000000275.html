Glove Box Lock Cylinder Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the glove box.
  1. Remove the glove box.
  1. Insert a hooked shaped tool in under the knob (A) while pulling it, and pull the top of the retainer (B) out of the slot (C).
    NOTE: Do not remove the retainer entirely. Leave the other side of it in the original position when removing and installing the lock cylinder.

  1. Reinstall the top of the retainer (A) in the original position, then reinstall the lock cylinder (B).
