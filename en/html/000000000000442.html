Windshield Rain Sensor Cover Base Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE: Wear eye protection while cutting the glass adhesive with piano wire.
  1. Carefully cut through the adhesive tape under the windshield rain sensor cover base (A) using the piano wire, then remove the base.
    NOTE: Take care not to damage the optical connector.
  1. Use a putty knife to scrape off all of the old adhesive tape from the windshield. Clean the inside face of the windshield with alcohol where new rain sensor cover is to be applied. Make sure the installing surface is kept free of water, oil and grease.
  1. Draw an alignment line (A) on the inside surface of the windshield around the optical connector (B) with a marker (C), which has a pen point with a width of 3 mm (0.01 in.).
  1. Apply primer on the double sided adhesive tape (D) of the rain sensor cover base (E). Let the primer dry at least 10 minutes, then attach it to the windshield within 12 hours.
  1. Align the cover base opening with an alignment mark you made in step 3, and center it, then press the adhesive portions into place securely.
