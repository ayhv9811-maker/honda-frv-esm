Dust and Pollen Filter Replacement
The dust and pollen filter should be replaced every 48,000 km (30,000 miles) or 24 months, whichever comes first. Replace the filter more often if the airflow is less than usual, or if the vehicle is driven in areas that have high concentrations of soot from industry or diesel powered vehicles.
NOTE: RHD type is shown, LHD type is symmetrical.
  1. Open the glove box. Remove the glove box stop on each side, then let the glove box hang down.
  1. Remove the filter lid (A) from the blower unit, then pull out the first dust and pollen filter (B). Slide the second filter (C) to the left, and pull it out.
  1. Remove the filter (A) from the housing (B), and replace the filter.
  1. Install the filter in the reverse order of removal. Make sure that there is no air leaking out of the evaporator.
