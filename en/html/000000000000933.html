Crankshaft Pulley Removal and Installation
Special Tools Required

Handle
 07JAB-001020B

Pulley holder attachment, HEX 50 mm
 07MAB-PY30100

Socket wrench, 19 mm
 07JAA-001020A
Removal
  1. Remove the right front tyres/wheels.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the alternator-A/C compressor belt.
  1. Remove the power steering (P/S) pump belt.
  1. Hold the pulley with holder handle (A) and holder attachment (B).
  1. Remove the bolt with a 19 mm socket (C) and breaker bar.
Installation
  1. Remove any oil or clean the pulleys (A), crankshaft (B), bolt (C), and washer (D). Lubricate new engine oil as shown.
  1. Install the crankshaft pulley, and tighten the bolt. Do not use an impact wrench.
    -1.
    Hold the pulley with the handle (A) and holder attachment (B), then tighten the bolt to 20 N·m (2.0 kgf·m, 14 lbf·ft) with a torque wrench and 19mm socket (C).
    -2.
    Mark (D) the bolt flange and crankshaft pulley by using the embossed marks (E) on the flange as shown.
    -3.
    Tighten the bolt an additional 90 ° (The next embossed mark (F) on the bolt flange line up with the mark on the crankshaft pulley).
  1. Install the power steering (P/S) pump belt.
  1. Install the alternator-A/C compressor belt.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Install the right front tyre/wheel.
