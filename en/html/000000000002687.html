MTF Leakage Check at Clutch Housing Cap
  1. Raise the vehicle, and make sure it is supported securely.
  1. Check for fluid leaks at the clutch housing cap (A).
  1. If there is a leak, remove the filler plug (A) and sealing washer (B).
  1. Remove the drain plug (C), and drain the transmission fluid (MTF).
  1. Reinstall the drain plug and a new sealing washer (D).
  1. Remove the clutch housing cap (A).
  1. Remove the O-ring (B) from the cap, and replace it.
  1. Install the new O-ring on the cap, and install the cap on the clutch housing (C).
  1. Refill the transmission with MTF.
