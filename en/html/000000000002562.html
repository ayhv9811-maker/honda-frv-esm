Starter Removal/Installation
Removal
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then write down the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the front grill cover.
  1. Remove the intake stay.
  1. Disconnect the hood switch connector (A), then remove the A/C condenser stay/rubber (B), radiator upper mount bracket/cushion (C), hood latch (D), left radiator upper mount bracket/cushion/rubber (E), and intercooler stay (F).
  1. Remove the bulkhead (A), then remove the harness clamps (B).
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the engine oil pressure switch.
  1. Remove the dipstick.
  1. Remove the vacuum hose (A), then remove the dipstick tube (B) and O-ring (C).
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A), then disconnect the radiator fan switch (High) connector (B), crankshaft position (CKP) sensor connector (C), and starter subharness connector (D).
  1. Disconnect the starter cable (A) from the B terminal, BLK/WHT wire (B) from the S terminal.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the starter, then remove the starter.
Installation
  1. Install the starter, then tighten the two bolts (A) securing the starter. Connect the starter cable (B) to the B terminal, BLK/WHT wire (C) to the S terminal.
  1. Make sure the crimped side of the ring terminal (A) is facing out.
  1. Install the harness clamp (A), then connect the radiator fan switch (High) connector (B), crankshaft position (CKP) sensor connector (C), and starter subharness connector (D).
  1. Install the dipstick tube (A) with new O-ring (B), then install the vacuum hose (C) and dipstick.
  1. Install the engine oil pressure switch.
  1. Install the engine cover.
  1. Install the harness clamps (A), then install the bulkhead (B). Apply touch-up paint to the bulkhead mounting bolts.
  1. Install the A/C condenser stay/rubber (A), radiator upper mount bracket/cushion (B), hood latch (C), left radiator upper mount bracket/cushion/rubber (D), and intercooler stay (E), then connect the hood switch connector (F).
  1. Install the intake stay.
  1. Adjust the hood latch alignment, refer to the Shop Manual, P/N 62SJD00.
  1. Install the from grill cover.
  1. Connect the positive cable to the battery first, and then connect the negative cable.
  1. Start the engine to make sure the starter works properly.
  1. Enter the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Do the power window control unit reset procedure, refer to the Shop Manual, P/N 62SJD00.
  1. Set the clock.
