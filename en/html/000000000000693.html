M/T Shift Arm Shaft Assembly Clearance Inspection
  1. Measure the clearance between the shift arm A (A) and select arm (B) with a feeler gauge (C). If the clearance exceeds the service limit, go to Step 2 .
    Standard:
    0.05−0.25 mm (0.002−0.010 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.5 mm (0.02 in.)

  1. Measure the diameter of the select arm pin.
    • If the diameter is not within the standard, replace the select arm.
    • If the diameter is within the standard, replace the shift arm A.
    Standard:
    7.9−8.0 mm (0.311−0.315 in.)

  1. Measure the clearance between the shift arm B (A) and shift arm C (B) with a feeler gauge (C). If the clearance exceeds the service limit, go to Step 4 .
    Standard:
    0.05−0.25 mm (0.002−0.010 in.)
    Service Limit:
    0.5 mm (0.02 in.)

  1. Measure the diameter of the shift arm C (B).
    • If the diameter is not within the standard, replace the shift arm C.
    • If the diameter is within the standard, replace the shift arm B.
    Standard:
    12.9−13.0 mm (0.508−0.512 in.)
