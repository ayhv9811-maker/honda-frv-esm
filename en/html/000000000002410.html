Rear Side Seat Cushion Cover Replacement - With Leather
Leather Seat Cushion Cover
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the rear side seat.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the dampers (A).
  1. Release the fastener (A) and rear hooks (B), and then unzip the seat cushion cover (C).
  1. Pull back the edge of the seat cushion cover (A) all the way around, and release the clips (B) from the seat cushion frame (C).
  1. Remove the seat cushion cover/pad (A) and the silencer (B) from the seat cushion frame (C).
  1. Release the clips (A) from under the seat cushion.
  1. Pull back the edge of the seat cushion cover all the way around, and release the clips (A), then separate the seat cushion cover (B) and pad.
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat cushion cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hooks and clips.
    • Using commercially available upholstery ring pliers (A), replace all of the clips you removed with new ones (B).
