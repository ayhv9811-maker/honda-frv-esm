IMT Valve Replacement
  1. Remove the intake manifold.
  1. Disconnect the vacuum hose (A).
  1. Remove the bolts (B).
  1. Remove the IMT actuator assembly (C).
    NOTE:
    • When installing the IMT actuator, make sure the bearing (D) is firmly secure into place if necessary.
    • Do not the IMT assembly interfere with the intake manifold (E).
    • When installing the IMT actuator, replace the O-rings (F).
  1. Install the IMT actuator assembly (A) so the hole (B) is visible.
  1. Turn the IMT actuator assembly (A), and set the bolt holes.
    NOTE: When turning the IMT actuator assembly, make sure the stopper (B) and groove (C) are fit.
