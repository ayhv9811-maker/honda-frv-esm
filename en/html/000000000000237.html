Cowl Cover Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the cowl cover and body.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  1. Remove the windshield wiper arms.
  1. From both sides of the cowl cover, release the hooks (A) from the cowl cover (B) and the front fender (C), then release the hood hinge cover (D) from roof molding (E), then remove the covers.
  1. Using the handy remover, detach the clips and washer tube clip (A) from the cowl cover (B). RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Detach the clips (A, B) by carefully pulling the windshield side edge of the cowl cover (C) upward, and release the forward hooks (D) by pulling the center cowl cover forward, then remove the cover.
  1. Install the covers in the reverse order of the removal, and note these items:
    • When installing both side hood hinge covers, take care not to damage there surfaces by the edges of both hood hinges.
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clip and hook portions into place securely.
