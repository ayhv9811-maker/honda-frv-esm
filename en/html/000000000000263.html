Side Defogger Vent Driver's Trim Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • RHD is shown; LHD is symmetrical.
  1. Pull the clip portion of the driver's side defogger vent trim (A) up by hand.
  1. Pull the vent trim out by releasing the hook (B).
  1. Reinstall the hook portion of the vent trim first, then securely push the clip into place.
