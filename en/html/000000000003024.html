  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect gauge control module connector A (20P).
  1. Check for continuity between gauge control module connector terminal A2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the key interlock solenoid and the gauge control module.■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Remove the following parts:
    • Dashboard center middle panel
    • Driver's middle panel
    • Center lower panel
  1. Disconnect the D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector.
  1. Check for continuity between D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector terminal No. 6 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector terminal No. 6 and the gauge control module.■
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Shift the shift lever into the P position.
  1. Check for continuity between D3 switch/park pin switch/A/T gear position indicator panel light connector terminals No. 2 and No. 6. Do not push the shift lever button.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Replace the park pin switch. ■
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Disconnect gauge control module connector C (16P).
  1. Shift the shift lever in any position other than P.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between gauge control module connector terminal C4 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V when the shift lever is in any position other than the P position, and no voltage when the shift lever is in the P position?
YES -
Substitute a known-good gauge control module and recheck.■
NO -
Repair open or short in the wire between the gauge control module and the transmission range switch.■
