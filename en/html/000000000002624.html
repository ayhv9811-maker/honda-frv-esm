Cowl Cover Insulator Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the cowl cover and body.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the speed nuts, then remove the cowl cover insulator (A) from the cowl cover (B). The driver's side is shown; the passenger's side is similar.
  1. Install the covers in the reverse order of the removal, and note these items:
    • Replace the speed nuts with new ones.
    • Push the speed nuts into place securely.
