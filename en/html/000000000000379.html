Rear Center Seat Cushion Cover/Pad Replacement - Without Leather
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the rear center seat.
  1. Life the seat cushion up, and disconnect the slide cables from both seat tracks.
  1. Pull both slide cables (A) out through the cable band (B).
  1. Release the fastener (C) and rear hooks (D), and unzip the seat cushion cover (E).
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the dampers (A).
  1. While pulling the slide lever (A), remove the screws securing the slide lever trim (B).
  1. Push the slide lever trim (A) as shown to release the trim (A) from the hooks (B), then remove the trim.
  1. Release the hook (A) at the slide lever portion, then pull out the seat cushion frame (B) from the seat cushion cover/pad (C), then remove the cover/pad.
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat cushion cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the clips and hooks.
    • Replace any cilps you removed with new ones. Install them with commercially available upholstery ring pliers.
