Timing Belt Drive Pulley Replacement
  1. Remove the timing belt.
  1. Remove the crankshaft position (CKP) sensor.
  1. Remove the timing belt drive pulley (A).
  1. Inspect the timing belt pulley for damage. Replace the timing belt drive pulley if it is damaged.
  1. IInstall the new timing belt drive pulley.
    NOTE: Install the timing belt guide plate (B) with the concave surface facing in.
  1. Install the CKP sensor.
  1. Install the timing belt.
