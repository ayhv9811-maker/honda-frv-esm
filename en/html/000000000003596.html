Headlight Adjuster Leveling Sensor Troubleshooting - With HID
With HID
Before testing, inspect the No. 16 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Turn the headlight switch ON.
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 3 terminal and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
An open in the wire or faulty headlight leveling control unit.■
  1. Check for voltage between the No. 1 terminal and body ground.
Is there 5 V or more?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
An open in the wire or body short.■
  1. Raise the vehicle, and make sure it is securely supported.
  1. Remove the headlight leveling sensor mounting nut from the control arm, and make the shaft move free.
  1. Measure voltage between the No. 6 [No. 7] and No. 12 [No. 4] terminals by moving the shaft slowly.
    [ ]: Rear
Is there about 0.5−4.5 V?
YES -
Check the headlight leveling control unit. ■
NO -
