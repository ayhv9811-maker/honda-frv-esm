Front Grille Replacement
NOTE:
  • When prying with a flat-tip screwdriver, wrap it with protective tape to prevent damage.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to scratch the front grille and front bumper.
  1. Remove the front bulkhead cover.
  1. Remove the clips. Release the hooks (A), then slide the front grille (B) forward to release the hooks (C) from the front bumper (D). Take care not to drop the clips inside the front bumper.
  1. If necessary, remove the screws, and release the hooks (A), then remove the upper grille molding (B), the center grille molding (C), the right lower grille molding (D), the left lower grille molding (E), and the front center emblem base (F) from the front grille base (G).
  1. Install the front grille in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Install the hook portions of the front grill into place securely.
