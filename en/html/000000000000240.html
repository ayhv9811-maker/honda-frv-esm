Roof Rail Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE:
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to scratch the covers, roof rail, and body.
  1. Remove the front cover (A) and rear cover (B) by releasing the tabs (C) then pulling them. Remove the center cap (D) and screws, then using a T30 Torx bit, remove the nuts (E) from the front bracket (F) and rear bracket (G).
  1. Remove the roof rail (H). Do not remove the rubber pads from the brackets.
  1. Remove the center cover (I). Using a T30 Torx bit, remove the nuts (E, J) and the center bracket (K). Do not remove the rubber pad from the center bracket.
  1. Install the roof rail in the reverse order of removal, and make sure the front and rear covers are snapped tightly into place.
