Sunroof Sunshade Replacement
  1. Remove the drip rail.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the set plate (A) from each side.
  1. Slide the sunshade (A) until you can see both front sunshade shoes (B).
  1. While lifting the front portion of the sunshade (A), move the sunshade forward until you can see both rear sunshade shoes (B). Do not damage the sunshade.
  1. Release both rear sunshade shoes from the guide rail portions of the frame, then remove the sunshade.
  1. If necessary, remove the screws securing the rear sunshade shoe (A) and holder (B) on the sunshade (C).
  1. Pry up at the gap between the holder (A) and the slider (B) so that a pin (C) comes off the slot (D), then remove the slider and the spring (E). Remove the stopper (F) from the holder.
  1. Install the sunshade in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Apply liquid thread lock to the set plate mounting screws, and sunshade rear shoe mounting screws before reinstallation.
    • Check the glass height adjustment.
  1. Check for water leaks. Let the water run freely from a hose without a nozzle. Do not use a high-pressure spray.
