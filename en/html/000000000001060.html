Rocker Arm Oil Control Solenoid (VTEC Solenoid Valve) Replacement
  1. Remove the resonator.
  1. Disconnect the VTEC solenoid valve 1P connector (A), VTEC oil pressure switch 2P connector (B).
  1. Remove the VTEC solenoid valve (C).
  1. Install the valve in the reverse order of removal with a new solenoid valve filter (D).
