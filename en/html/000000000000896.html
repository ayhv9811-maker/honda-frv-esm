Water Outlet/EGR Passage Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A), then remove the resonator (B).
  1. Remove the thermostat.
  1. Remove the ground cables (A) and harness clamp bracket mounting bolt (B).
  1. Remove the upper radiator hose (A), heater hose (B), water bypass hose (C), and connecting pipe (D).
  1. Disconnect the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve connector engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor connector.
  1. Remove the EGR valve (A) and ECT sensor (B), then remove the water outlet/EGR passage (C).
  1. Install the water outlet/EGR passage (A) with new gasket (B).
  1. Install the ECT sensor (C) with new O-ring (D).
  1. Install the EGR valve (E) with new gasket (F).
  1. Connect the EGR valve connector and ECT sensor connector.
  1. Install the connecting pipe (A) with new O-ring (B).
  1. Install the upper radiator hose (C), heater hose (D), and water bypass hose (E).
  1. Install the ground cables (A) and harness clamp bracket mounting bolt (B).
  1. Install the thermostat.
  1. Install the resonator (A), then install the harness clamp (B).
  1. Refill the radiator with engine coolant, and bleed air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
