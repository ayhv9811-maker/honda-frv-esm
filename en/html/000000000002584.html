Intermediate Shaft Disassembly
Special Tools Required

Oil seal driver
 07947-SB00100

Half shaft base
 07NAF-SR30101
  1. Remove the set ring (A), outer seal (B), and external snap ring (C).
  1. Press the intermediate shaft (A) out of the intermediate shaft bearing (B) using a press. Be careful not to damage the metal rings (C) on the intermediate shaft during disassembly.
  1. Remove the internal snap ring.
  1. Press the intermediate shaft bearing (A) out of the bearing support (B) using the special tools and a press.
