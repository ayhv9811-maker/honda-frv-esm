Parking Brake Cable Replacement

Exploded View
NOTE:
  • Release the parking lever all the way back before replacing the parking lever assembly.
  • The parking brake cable must not be bent or distorted. This will lead to stiff operation and premature cable failure. Refer to the Exploded View as needed during this procedure.
Parking brake cable A
  1. Remove the left-front side seat, then lift up the floor carpet at the insulator cutout.
  1. Remove the equalizer cover (A) and mid cover (B).
  1. Remove the brake hose clip (C).
  1. Push the lever (B) forward to loose the parking brake cable A to remove its cable end (C).
  1. Remove the center lower cover.
  1. Turn the 6 mm bolt (B) counterclockwise about 3 times to loose the parking brake cable A from the parking lever assembly (C).
  1. Remove the parking switch connector (D), and three 8 mm flange bolts.
  1. Gently pull out the parking lever assembly, then remove the cable end (B) of the parking brake cable A.
    NOTE: For some types, move the parking lever shaft (C) by using a flat-tipped screw driver as shown to remove the cable end.
  1. Install the parking lever assembly in the reverse order of removal, and note following items:
    • The parking brake cable A must not be bent or distorted. This will lead to stiff operation and premature cable failure.
    • After replacing the new parking brake cable A, operate the parking brake with 196 N (20 kgf, 44 lbf) of force to fully apply 10 times before adjusting the parking brake.
Parking brake cable
  1. Remove the parking brake shoes, and disconnect the parking brake cable from the brake shoe.
  1. Remove the parking brake cable mounting bolts from the backing plate (A), and remove the parking brake cable (B).
  1. Install the parking brake cable in the reverse order of removal, and adjust the parking brake. Apply the parking brake firmly 10 times, then adjust it again.
