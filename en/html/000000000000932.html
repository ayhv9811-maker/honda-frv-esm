Timing Belt Inspection
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
  1. Remove the grommet from the upper cover, and disconnect the camshaft position (CMP) sensor connector, then remove the upper cover.
  1. Inspect the timing belt for cracks and oil or coolant contamination. Replace the belt if it is oil or coolant contaminated. Remove any oil or solvent that gets on the belt.
  1. Loosen the crankshaft pulley bolt, then retighten the pulley bolt.
