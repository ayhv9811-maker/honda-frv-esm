DTC Troubleshooting: 12, 14, 16, 18
DTC 12, 14, 16, 18:
 Wheel Sensor (Electrical Noise/Intermittent Interruption)
NOTE: If the ABS indicator comes on because of electrical noise, the indicator will go off when you drive the vehicle over 15 km/h (9 mph) and the noise is gone.
  1. Inspect the appropriate wheel sensor and magnetic encoder.
