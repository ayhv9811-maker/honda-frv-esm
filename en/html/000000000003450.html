Air Cleaner Removal/Installation
  1. Disconnect the MAF sensor/IAT sensor connector (A).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing cover (B).
  1. Remove the air cleaner element (C) from the air cleaner housing (D).
  1. Loosen the bolt (E), and the pipe (F).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing cover.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
