Fuel Pump/Fuel Gauge Sending Unit Replacement
Special Tools Required

Fuel sender wrench
Â 07AAA-S0XA100
  1. Relieve the fuel pressure.
  1. Remove the fuel fill cap.
  1. Raise the rear center seat.
  1. Remove the access panel (A) from the floor.
  1. Disconnect the fuel pump 5P connector (B).
  1. Disconnect the quick-connect fitting (C) from the fuel tank unit.
  1. Using the special tool, loosen the fuel tank unit locknut (A).
  1. Remove the fuel tank unit locknut (A) and the fuel tank unit.
  1. Remove the reservoir (B) and remove the fuel filter (C), the fuel gauge sending unit (D), and the fuel pressure regulator (E).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal with a new base gasket (F), new fuel tank unit locknut, new O-rings (G), then check these items:
    • When connecting the wire harness, make sure the connection is secure and the connector (H) is firmly locked into the place.
    • When installing the fuel gauge sending unit, make sure the connection is secure and the connector is firmly locked into place. Be careful not to bend or twist the connector excessively.
    • When installing the fuel tank unit, align the marks (I) on the fuel tank (J) and the fuel tank unit (K).
