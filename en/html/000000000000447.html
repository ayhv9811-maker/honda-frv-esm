Rear Window Wiper Motor Replacement
  1. Remove the cover (A), mounting nut (B), and rear window wiper arm (C).
  1. Open the tailgate and remove the tailgate trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the 4P connector (A) from the rear window wiper motor (B).
  1. Remove the mounting bolts and the wiper motor.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
    • Grease the moving parts.
    • Check the rear window wiper motor operation.
