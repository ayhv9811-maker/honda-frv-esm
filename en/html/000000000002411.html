Rear Center Seat Cushion Cover/Pad Replacement - With Leather
Leather Seat Cushion Cover
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the rear center seat.
  1. Lift the seat cushion up, and disconnect the slide cables from both seat tracks.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the dampers (A).
  1. While pulling the slide lever (A), remove the screws securing the slide lever trim (B).
  1. Push the slide lever trim (A) as shown to release the trim from the hooks (B), then remove the trim.
  1. Pull both slide cables (A) out through the cable band (B).
  1. Release the fastener (C) and rear hooks (D), and unzip the seat cushion cover (E). Pull back the cover, then release the hook (F).
  1. Release the rear portion of the seat cushion cover (A) from the seat cushion frame (B).
  1. Pull out the seat cushion cover/pad (A) from the seat cushion frame (B), then release the hook (C) at the slide lever portion, then remove the cover/pad and silencer (D).
  1. Release the clips (A) from under the seat cushion.
  1. Pull back the edge of the seat cushion cover all the way around, and release the clips (A), then separate the seat cushion cover (B) and pad.
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat cushion cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hooks and clips.
    • Using commercially available upholstery ring pliers (A), replace all of the clips you removed with new ones (B).
