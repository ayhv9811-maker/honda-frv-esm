1. Remove the dashboard lower panel.
  1. Disconnect the integrated control unit connector B (16P) from the integrated control unit.
  1. Remove the integrated control unit.
  1. Inspect the connector and socket terminals to be sure they are all marking contact.
    • If the terminals are bent, loose or corroded, repair them necessary, and recheck the system.
    • If the terminals look OK, go to Step 5 .
  1. With the connector still disconnected, make these input tests at the connector.
    • If any test indicates a problem, find and correct the cause, then recheck the system.
    • If all the input tests prove OK, replace the integrated control unit.
