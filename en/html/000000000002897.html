A/T 4th and 5th Clutch Disassembly
Special Tools Required

Clutch spring compressor set
 07LAE-PX40000

Clutch spring compressor attachment
 07LAE-PX40100

Clutch spring compressor attachment
 07HAE-PL50101

Clutch spring compressor bolt assembly
  07GAE-PG40200

Snap ring pliers
 07LGC-0010100
  1. Remove the snap ring with a screwdriver.
  1. Remove the clutch end plate (A), clutch discs (B) (4), clutch wave-plates (C) (4), and waved spring (D) from the 4th clutch drum (E).
  1. Remove the clutch end plate (A), clutch discs (B) (4), clutch wave-plates (C) (4), and waved spring (D) from the 5th clutch drum (E).
  1. Install the special tools.
  1. Be sure the special tool (A) is adjusted to have full contact with the return spring retainer (B).
  1. If either end of the special tool is set over an area of the spring retainer that is unsupported by the return spring, the retainer may be damaged.
  1. Compress the return spring until the snap ring can be removed.
  1. Remove the snap ring with the snap ring pliers.
  1. Remove the special tools.
  1. Remove the snap ring (A), spring retainer (B), and return spring (C).
  1. Wrap a shop rag around the clutch drum, and apply air pressure to the fluid passage to remove the piston. Place a finger tip on the other passage while applying air pressure.
  1. Remove the clutch piston (A), and remove the outer O-ring (B) and inner O-ring (C) from the piston.
