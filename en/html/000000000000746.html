DTC Troubleshooting: P0401 (80)
DTC P0401:
 EGR Insufficient Flow
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 3,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Do the EGR TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
Is the result OK?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Test-drive under these conditions:
    • Engine coolant temperature above 70 °C (158 °F)
    • Transmission in 4th gear
    • Vehicle speed at 40 km/h (25 mph) or more, and engine speed between 1,100 and 3,000 rpm (min −1 )
    • During the drive, decelerate (with the throttle fully closed) for 5 seconds
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0401 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Clean any carbon build-up on the EGR valve with carburetor cleaner.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the EGR valve.
  1. Clean the intake manifold EGR port with carburetor cleaner. Also, clean the passage inside the EGR valve with carburetor cleaner.
  1. Install the EGR valve.
  1. Do the EGR TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
Is the result OK?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the EGR valve.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (ll).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Do the ECM idle learn procedure.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine speed at 3,000 rpm (min −1 ) without load (in neutral) until the radiator fan comes on, then let it idle.
  1. Test-drive under these conditions:
    • Engine coolant temperature above 70 °C (158 °F)
    • Transmission in 4th gear
    • Vehicle speed at 40 km/h (25 mph) or more, and engine speed between 1,100 and 3,000 rpm (min −1 )
    • During the drive, decelerate (with the throttle fully closed) for 5 seconds
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P0401 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the EGR valve and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
