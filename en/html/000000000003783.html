Fuel Filter Water Bleeding
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Place a suitable container under the fuel filter to catch up the water.
  1. Connect the hose (A) to water screw (B).
  1. Loosen the water screw, and bleed the water from the fuel filter.
  1. Tighten the water screw securely, and disconnect the hose.
  1. Start the engine, and then check the water in diesel filter warning indicator (A).
    • If the water in diesel filter warning indicator is on, go to Step 7 .
    • If the water in diesel filter warning indicator is not on, fuel filter water bleeding is complete.
  1. Disconnect the fuel filter water level switch 2P connector, and then recheck the water in diesel filter warning indicator.
    • If the water in diesel filter warning indicator is on, repair short in the wire between the fuel filter water level switch and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 86. If wire is OK, substitute a known-good ECM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good ECM, replace the original ECM.
    • If the water in diesel filter warning indicator is not on, replace the fuel filter water level switch.
  1. Bleed the fuel system.
