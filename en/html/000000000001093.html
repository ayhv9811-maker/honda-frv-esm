Alternator and Regulator Circuit Troubleshooting
  1. Be sure the battery is sufficiently charged and in good condition.
  1. Raise the hoist to full height.
  1. Hook up the ammeter, 0−400 A, to the starter subharness.
  1. Lower the hoist.
  1. Hook up the voltmeter, 0−20 V (accurate within 0.1 V), to T101.
  1. Start the engine. Hold the engine at 3,000 rpm (min −1 ) with no load shift lever in Neutral until the radiator fan comes on then let it idle.
  1. Raise the engine speed to 2,000 rpm (min −1 ) and hold it there.
  1. Turn the headlights (high beam) on, and measure voltage at the under-hood fuse/relay box terminal.
Is the voltage between 13.9 and 15.1 V?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Replace the alternator or repair the alternator. ■
