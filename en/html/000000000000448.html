Wiper/Washer Switch Test/Replacement
  1. Remove the steering column covers.
  1. Disconnect the 14P connector (A) from the wiper/washer switch (B).
  1. Remove the two screws, then pull out the wiper/washer switch.
  1. Inspect the connector terminals to be sure they are all making good contact.
    • If the terminals are bent, loose or corroded, repair them as necessary, and recheck the system.
    • If the terminals look OK, check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the tables. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the switch.
