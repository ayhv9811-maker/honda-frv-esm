Engine Oil Replacement
  1. Remove the drain bolt (A), and drain the engine oil.
  1. Reinstall the drain bolt with a new washer (B).
  1. Refill with the recommended oil
    Capacity
    At Oil Change:
    3.3 L (3.5 US qt, 2.9 Imp qt)
    At Oil Change including Filter:
    3.5 L (3.7 US qt, 3.1 Imp qt)
    After Engine Overhaul:
    4.2 L (4.4 US qt, 3.7 Imp qt)

  1. Run the engine for more than 3 minutes, then check for oil leaks.
